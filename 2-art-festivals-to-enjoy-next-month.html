2 Art Festivals to Enjoy Next Month

Howard Alan Events and American Craft Endeavors will keep the festival season going by staging two art shows in Martin County next month.

The first, the twenty-third annual Hobe Sound Festival of the Arts, returns February 3 and 4, from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. both days. More than 250 top artists and skilled craft artisans from around the country will set up hundreds of gallery-style booths filled with every medium of fine art lining Dixie Highway from Bridge Road to Venus Street. The festival will take place at 11954 SE Dixie Highway, Hobe Sound, FL  33455.

On February 24 and 25, head to Stuart for the thirty-fourth annual Downtown Stuart Art Festival. Seaside surrounds will set the scene for the longest-running artistic events of the year. Residents and visitors will spend the day appreciating larger-than-life sculptures, glittering glass, jewelry, photography, and more from nearly 200 national artists in this juried showcase. The festival will take place at 26 SW Osceola St., Stuart, FL 34994.

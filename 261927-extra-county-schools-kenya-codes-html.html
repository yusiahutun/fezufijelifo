How many types of high schools are there in Kenya?

Schools in Kenya are divided into four main categories: national, extra county, county, and sub-county secondary schools.

County schools fall below extra-county schools in the national school ranking.

There are hundreds of extra county schools in Kenya. These institutions are part of the four-tier secondary school system in the country. Formerly known as provincial schools, these institutions are centres of academic excellence and often give national schools a run for their money.

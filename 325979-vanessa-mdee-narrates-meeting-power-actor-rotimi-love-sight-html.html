Vanessa Mdee narrates meeting Power actor Rotimi, says it was love at first sight

Sensational Tanzanian songstress, Vanessa Mdee, has for the first time shared details of her new love life with Power Series star Rotimi.

Mdee was in a long time relationship with Tanzanian singer Juma Jux before meeting her new star lover.

In an interview with a Tanzanian vlogger Millard Ayo, the singer revealed she met Rotimi during the Essence Festivals and theirs was love at first sight.

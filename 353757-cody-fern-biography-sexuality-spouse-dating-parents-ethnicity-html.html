He used the money from the scholarship to relocate to Los Angeles, California, in the United States of America with the hope of getting more gigs and improving his skills.

Cody Fern from House of Cards had his breakthrough after playing the role of Jim Mason in the film The Tribes of Palos Verdes. Later on, he acted as the director of a short movie dubbed Pisces. Since then, he has continued to be one of the best actors the American film sector has seen.

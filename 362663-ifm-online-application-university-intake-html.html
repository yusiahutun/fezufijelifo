IFM online application for 2024/2025 university intake

The Institute of Finance Management (IFM) is the oldest higher-learning financial institution in Tanzania. IFM offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in financial management and business-related courses. Discover the IFM online application process today.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

IFM has different campuses in different parts of Tanzania. The institute enrols thousands of undergraduate and postgraduate students every year. It accepts online applications. The IFM online application process is simple and straightforward.

IFM online application: all you need to know

IFM courses and qualifications are offered on the institute's campuses. The main campus is located in Dar es Salaam. The other campuses are located in Dodoma, Mwanza, and Simiyu. Below are all the details you should know about the institute.

IFM online application process 2024/2025

Follow the steps below to complete the online application process.

  • Visit the IFM website on an internet-enabled device.
  • Click Study at IFM on the dashboard, then Online Application.
  • Next, click Apply for the indicated intake.
  • Create an application account.

How do you pay your IFM application fee?

You can visit any branch or bank agent of CRDB, NBC, TPB, NMB, or BOT to pay the application fee. Alternatively, you can use Mobile Network Operators (MNO).

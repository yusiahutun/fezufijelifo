Kikuyu elder warns men against sucking their wives, girlfriends' breasts, says it's a curse

- The Kikuyu elder said men who had sucked their women's breasts needed to undergo cleansing to obliterate curses linked to the act

- The old man said women whose men suck their breasts automatically become their husband's mothers

- He warned men will lose control over their homes and women will subdue them if they continue engaging in such an act

A Kikuyu Council of Elder identified as King'e Wabeti has warned men against sucking their wives and girlfriends' breasts saying it is an abomination.

The elder said in the old times, a Kikuyu man did not suck his wife's breasts because it automatically made the woman his mother who could undermine him in the family.

In a video, King'e said a woman's breasts were meant for a child to suckle but not for grown men and warned such an act attracts curses.

"That woman whom you will suck her breast, she has become your mother and you cannot control her at home. That is why many homes are crumbling because the man is sleeping with his mother," he said.

The elderly man added that women who were giving their men their busts were deliberately doing so to subdue them and bewitch them to be fools.

"That is why you find that in this country when people get married in a grand wedding, their marriage barely makes it to two years because that man is sleeping with his mother because of sucking her breasts," he added.

The elder further added there was a need to continue observing traditions because in generations to come, women will start ruling over men and they will have no say.

"This is because he will be bewitched after sucking the breasts," he said.

Kinge'i said men who have sucked their wives or girlfriends' bosoms should go through cleansing to eliminate the curse linked with the act.

The Kikuyu Council of Elders and Murang’a Cultural Association observed the county's traditions were hanging perilously on the cliff due to increased cases of same-sex relationships.

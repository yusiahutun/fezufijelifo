Top 10 richest female wrestlers and their net worth in 2022

Several years ago, there was no space for women in the world of wrestling as men dominated all aspects of the billion-dollar industry. That has since changed because, in the current era, many female wrestlers command the same respect as their male counterparts. There is still a lot to be done to bridge the gap present in the income disparities between male and female wrestlers. That said, the richest female wrestlers have a fortune that will convince other women to join this career.

The increase in the reception of female wrestlers by fans, coupled with the continued promotion of female wrestling events has resulted in a significant rise in female wrestlers' paychecks. Events such as WWE All-Women Pay-Per-View and Raw Women Championship have created worldwide stars like Alexa Bliss and Ronda Rousey.

Ten richest female wrestlers 2022

Women have shown that WWE is not a sport where only men can excel. They too have what it takes to step inside the ring and give audiences a good entertainment. Here are the top 10 richest wrestlers according to their current net worth.

1. Ronda Rousey - $13 million

Who is the richest female wrestler? Ronda Jean Rousey has been one of the most successful inside and outside the ring. She is not only a professional wrestler but also an actress, author, former mixed martial artist and judoka.

She first signed a WWE contract in 2018 with an estimated annual salary of about $2.1 million. Ronda first won an Olympic bronze medal in Judo at the Summer Olympics of 2018. This made her the first female American Judo athlete to win an Olympic medal. According to Wealthy Gorilla, her net worth is $13 million in 2021.

2. Brie Bella - $12 million

Brie Bella is the half of the Bella Twins tag team duo. She is a very accomplished wrestler individually having won the WWE Divas Champion title. She also happens to be one of the hottest WWE Divas in the world.

Christmas at the Plaza Hallmark cast profiles and photos

For centuries, Christmas in New York City has always been exceptional. It is a time when everything in the Big Apple lights up, including the magical decorations of Washington Square Park. As you walk through the streets of New York, you feel a sense of love in the air. So, New York City was the perfect place for filming Christmas at the Plaza, a film with a charming story incorporating traditional family values at Christmas. But one thing that made the 2019 film worth binge-watching today is how the chemistry between the cast members brought the entire story to life.

Christmas at the Plaza is a 2019 romantic film that revolves around Jessica. Jessica is an archival historian trapped in a relationship that seems to be declining fast. She gets a job to make an exhibition that honours the Christmas tree and its history at the Plaza Hotel.

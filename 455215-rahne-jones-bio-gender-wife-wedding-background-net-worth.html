Early life

Rahne Jones was born on 4 May 1987 (age 35 years as of 2022) in Silver Spring, Maryland, United States. Her father’s name is Ronnie, after whom she is named. Her parents took her to Paint Branch High School. She played basketball while in high school.

Jones graduated from high school in 2005 and joined Radford University. She went to university on afull basketball scholarship for her freshman year. However, she did not finish her studies at Radford University. Although she had joined the institution on a basketball scholarship, she gave it up. After studying at the University of Maryland, Rahne graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in English. She then joined the labour force and started working as an inspector for the US Department of Homeland Security.

Career beginnings

Rahne started her career in the entertainment industry in 2019. But before she became an actress, she used to work at the US Department of Homeland Security as an inspector. She spent four and a half years at Homeland Security and worked with agencies of various departments, including TSA, ICE, and CBP, ensuring that the programmes initiated secured the homeless.

Rahne quit the job she once considered rewarding and moved to New York City. She found herself broke and slept on couches. She then worked at a store and sold hats. Soon afterwards, she started going to theatres and involved herself with acting.

Rise to fame

In 2019, she auditioned for a role on The Politician, a comedy-drama series on Netflix. It was her first time auditioning for a role in a TV series. Luckily, she got the role two weeks later when the producers cast her in the main role as Skye Leighton. Jones landed the role despite not having prior experience acting in a film, television, or streaming show.

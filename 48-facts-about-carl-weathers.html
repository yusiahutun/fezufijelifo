Yes, Carl Weathers has received recognition for his acting talent. He won the NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for his role in The Predator and was also nominated for a Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actor for his portrayal of Greef Karga in The Mandalorian.

Yes, before his acting career, Carl Weathers played professional football in the NFL for the Oakland Raiders. He was a linebacker and participated in Super Bowl II before shifting his focus to acting.

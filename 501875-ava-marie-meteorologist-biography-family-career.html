Ava Marie's biography

The prominent American meteorologist was born and raised alongside her two sisters in Mile High, Denver, Colorado, United States of America. Ava is a private individual, as she has not disclosed more details about her date of birth and the names of her parents and siblings.

Ava studied cinematography and played football and softball while in high school. Although she has not disclosed to the public the high school, she attended. She graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Atmospheric Science from the University of Kansas.

Career

Ava Marie is a well-known meteorologist and journalist. She reportedly developed a passion for weather due to snowstorms and thunderstorms associated with Denver, Colorado, where she grew up. She began working at a local recreational facility's front desk, where she used to process memberships and coached kids in their sports.

She later worked as an intern at KMBC, a sister station for WBAL Hearst based in Kansas City. While there, she used to forecast tornadoes. Later she was hired by the 6 News in Lawrence, Kansas as a meteorologist.

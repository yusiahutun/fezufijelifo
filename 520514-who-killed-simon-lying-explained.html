What happened to Simon Kelleher?

Simon meets a quick and untimely demise during the first episode. According to CBR, he entered detention with Bronwyn, Addy, Cooper, and Nate. He appeared utterly healthy when he entered the detention.

Everything was normal until he poured himself a glass of water from a water cooler. He then took a sip of the water and went into anaphylactic shock. The other students rushed to get him an EpiPen, but they did not find any in his backpack or the nurse's office.

They had no choice but to watch helplessly as Simon struggled to breathe. The ambulance was called, but unfortunately, Simon died in hospital.

How did Jake kill Simon in One of Us Is Lying

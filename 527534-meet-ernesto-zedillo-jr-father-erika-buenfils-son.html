Ernesto Zedillo Jr's biography

The businessman was born sometime in 1974 to Nilda Patricia Velasco and Ernesto Zedillo Ponce de León. He is 49 years old (as of 2023) and is of Hispanic descent. Zedillo Jr has four siblings: Emiliano, Carlos, Nilda Patricia and Rodrigo.

Career

Ernesto Zedillo Jr. has had a diverse career spanning multiple sectors. He is known as an entrepreneur and architect, involved in architectural works in various construction companies. Some of his alleged roles in construction include the Veracruz Tower and a tourist megaproject in Cozumel, Quintana Roo.

Ernesto Zedillo Jr.'s wife

The businessman is married to journalist and TV host Rebeca Sáenz. The couple met sometime in late 2003 during the inauguration of the restaurant Vaova, of which Zedillo Jr. was a partner.

They began dating in mid-February 2004 and engaged on 22 October of the same year. On 8 January 2005, the couple married in Los Cabos, Mexico. Ernesto Jr and Rebeca have two daughters, Isabel and Victoria.

Ernesto Zedillo Jr's divorce

As of November 2023, no publicly available information indicates that Zedillo Jr. and his wife, Rebeca Sáenz, have divorced.

Ernesto Zedillo Jr and Erika Buenfil

According to MDZ, Ernesto Jr. had a brief relationship with Mexican actress Erika Buenfil in 2004. The pair met at the Beibi' O bowling alley in Acapulco, Guerrero, where Erika reportedly rejected the businessman's advances mainly because of their age difference.

However, they reportedly decided to give their fling a chance after meeting again at a bar in Mexico City and a private party on a yacht.

After a two-month relationship in 2004, Erika discovered her pregnancy. She informed him, but he didn't want to be involved. Eight months into her pregnancy, Ernesto got married to journalist Rebeca Sáenz.

One month later, on 2 February 2005, Erika Buenfil gave birth to her son, Nicolás de Jesús Buenfil López.

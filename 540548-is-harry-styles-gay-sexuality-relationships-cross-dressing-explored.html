Is Harry Styles gay?

What is Harry Styles' sexual orientation? Styles has never confirmed his sexuality publicly despite heavy speculation from critics and fans. He is accused of borrowing typically queer characteristics as he’s often seen wearing feather boas while at the same time not openly talking about his sexuality. That is why some people think he is gay.

Over the years, Harry has sent mixed signals about his sexuality. For instance, in an interview with British GQ magazine’s September 2013 issue, Harry stated that he was “pretty sure” he didn’t identify as bisexual.

Later, in 2014, Harry commented that he was interested in dating someone and being female was “not that important”. Then came 2017, and the talented singer stated he “never felt the need to label his sexuality”.

In 2018, Harry Styles released a track called Medicine that fans labelled as a bisexual theme. A few months later, Harry was quoted stating, “We’re all a little bit gay, aren’t we?”

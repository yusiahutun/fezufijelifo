Harmonize Masterfully Braids Lover Poshy Queen's Hair in Lovely Video

  • Tanzanian singer Harmonize lovingly made his lover Poshy Queen's hair in a romantic video
  • The star carefully parted her hair with a comb before braiding it into neat cornrows as Poshy just sat impressed by his skills
  • The video elicited mixed reactions among netizens, with some delighted by Harmonize's gesture and others saying he was doing too much

Tanzanian singer Harmonize has showcased his hairdressing talents by skillfully styling his girlfriend Poshy Queen’s hair.

How did Harmonize style Poshy Queen's hair?

A charming video, shared on both Harmonize and Poshy’s Instagram accounts, captured the romantic moment.

The clip showed Poshy seated with a towel draped around her shoulders.

Meanwhile, Hamonize, dressed in a singlet and short, stood behind her and meticulously crafted each cornrow.

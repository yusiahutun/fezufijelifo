The Commerzbank-Arena is located in Frankfurt, Germany.

The Commerzbank-Arena, commonly known as the Waldstadion, is situated in Frankfurt, Germany. This iconic stadium has a rich history and plays host to various sporting and cultural events.

It has a seating capacity of over 51,000.

The Commerzbank-Arena can accommodate more than 51,000 spectators, making it one of the largest stadiums in Germany. With its impressive size, it provides an electrifying atmosphere for fans during matches and events.

The stadium was initially built for the 2006 FIFA World Cup.

The construction of the Commerzbank-Arena was primarily for the purpose of hosting matches during the 2006 FIFA World Cup. It served as a venue for several exciting games, including the semi-final between Germany and Italy.

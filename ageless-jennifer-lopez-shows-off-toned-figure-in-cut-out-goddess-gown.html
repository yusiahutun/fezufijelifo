'Ageless' Jennifer Lopez shows off toned figure in cut-out 'goddess' gown

Jennifer Lopez just dropped another age-defying showstopper of an Instagram post and fans can’t believe their eyes. Many are hailing the On The Floor singer a “goddess,” while others had “no words” after seeing JLo in a black cut-out dress.

The songstress and actress appeared to be all smiles as she attended a glamorous Las Vegas event on November 17 with her husband, Ben Affleck. JLo is renowned for her toned physique, and she has her followers in total awe as she enjoys her mid-fifties.

Jennifer stuns in cut-out dress

Movie star of Maid in Manhattan and Hustlers, Jennifer Lopez, 54, stepped out and graced The Venetian Resort Las Vegas with her presence on Friday night.

Attending the 2023 Eastern Congo Initiative Poker and Blackjack Tournament, JLo stunned in a black cut-out dress from David Koma.

The star paired the showstopping gown with a black clutch bag from Rodo.

Jennifer’s outfit has a hefty price tag with her dress currently priced at £2,256 online, and her purse costing £790.

Everything down to the finer details were taken care of for the All I Have singer’s Las Vegas look. Her nail artist, Tom Bachik, is tagged in the post, as well as high-end jewelry brand REZA.

JLo opted for a nude manicure and REZA’s “Spirale” earrings which are worth a whopping $40k.

JLo fans left ‘breathless’

It’s safe to say that JLo’s recent Instagram posts have left her fans and followers gobsmacked.

Many took to the comments section to write that she looks: “Ageless.”

More agreed, writing: “Can’t believe she’s 54yrs old… Always looking beautiful and stunning…”

Others hailed the singer a “goddess,” a “beauty,” and a “gorgeous queen.”

Some people said that they were “literally speechless” at the Las Vegas post and one left a comment: “…Leaving half the fandom breathless.”

When it comes to the star’s workout regime and diet, her trainer, Dodd Romero, lifted the lid on what she does to stay in shape in a 2019 interview with Oprah Daily.

She reportedly trains for an hour a day five days a week and avoids alcohol and caffeine.

The actress is also said to sometimes go through ‘no carb’ and ‘no sugar’ periods but will allow herself a “cheat day” once a week.

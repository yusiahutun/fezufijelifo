Alicia Keys put on a fashionable display this weekend during an island vacation with husband Swizz Beatz.

The 42-year-old songstress — who was on tour this summer — showed off her fit figure in a patterned bikini top and matching coral shorts.

During one moment she and her 45-year-old producer beau held hands as they walked along a beach in Formentera, Spain.

The mother-of-two soaked up the sun in her low-cut swimwear top and high-waisted bottoms as they were joined by a female family member.

It comes as the couple celebrates Swizz's, born Kasseem Daoud Dean, 45th birthday, which was September 13.

The New York-bred music artist wore her dark curly hair in a high ponytail with singular braids over each of her ears.

She accented the long plaits with four chunky beads added at the ends - brown on one side and clear on the other.

She accessorized further with heart-shaped gold bamboo earrings and a double chain link necklace.

The star carried a large black tote bag over her arm that had 'New York or nowhere' printed across it in large white letters.

She added a pair of black cat eye shaped sunglasses to the seaside ensemble and carried black flip-flops in her hand.

Alicia went makeup-free, opting to show off her natural beauty as she enjoyed the company of her loved ones.

For his part, Swizz rocked a tan and black patterned co-ord consisting of a long-sleeved button-up shirt and lounge pants.

He rolled up the sleeves of his shirt and left the top buttons undone, showing his tattooed chest as well as two long necklaces.

On Saturday the Fallin' hitmaker took to Instagram to reflect on her husband as she paid a birthday tribute to him.

Engaging her 26.9 million followers, she uploaded a recap of his birthday celebration, which was attended by Pharrell and other close friends.

Alicia poured her heart out in the accompanying caption as she said: 'My love! There are no words to describe this special year and how special YOU are!! What a night. What a blessing to be with our family in places none of us could’ve ever imagined.'

The note to her spouse continued, 'The most amazing part about you, to me, is that you give SO much, ALL the time, to SO many. The way everyone celebrated you and shared their stories of how you have transformed and touched their lives made it the most genuine and unforgettable night!

Rarely does one get back what they give but this night just might have done it.

Thank you with all my heart to everyone that came from all across the globe to celebrate a man that deserve ALL his flowers.'

Then she said to her husband, who she married in 2010, 'I love you all with all my heart!'

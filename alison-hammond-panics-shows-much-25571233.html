Alison Hammond panics as she 'shows too much chest' in swimsuit for This Morning dip

Alison Hammond jumped into a freezing cold lake on ITV's This Morning - but she was left worrying about a serious bikini malfunction afterwards.

The hilarious ITV presenter had just dived into the chilly lake in Jeris, Lapland, during a festive edition of the family-friendly daytime show.

However, when she eventually climbed out of the water post-dip, she started panicking as she thought she was showing too much of her boobs.

In a desperate attempt to hide her assets from the cameras, Alison scrambled to adjust her red bikini as she cried out: "I'm showing too much juice!"

Anne Hathaway Wears Bright Yellow Short Shorts &amp; Unbuttoned Dress

After a breathtaking debut at the 75th Cannes Film Festival, Anne Hathaway's bold and elegant style returned yesterday for another showstopping moment.

Arriving at a Paris event unveiling Bulgari's newest High Jewellery Collection, the WeCrashed star wore a knockout fluorescent yellow look straight from Valentino's spring/summer 2022 Rendez-Vous runway. She pulled a sweeping long-sleeved dress from the collection and wore it unbuttoned to expose a pair of matching short shorts. For shoes, she opted for heeled sandals in a champagne shade.

As a Bulgari global ambassador, Hathaway also repped the brand by wearing a dazzling diamond choker necklace that featured a circular pendant with fringe.

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

Fellow brand ambassadors Priyanka Chopra and Lalisa Manobal, a.k.a. Lisa, were also in attendance. Chopra looked glamorous in a glittering burnt orange gown from Rasario, while Lisa wore a yellow set from Korean brand Pinkong.

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

Anne Hathaway, Priyanka Chopra Jonas, and Lalisa Manobal, a.k.a. Lisa, at the Bulgari event.

Bulgari Eden the Garden of Wonders, the latest luxe collection from the jewelry brand, includes more than 140 one-of-a-kind pieces and high-end watches that aim to celebrate the gems of nature.

For the first time in the brand's history, the collection features 30 creations dedicated to the emerald. "This stunning tribute to the Maison's long-standing affinity with the exquisite gemstone boasts some of the most extraordinary wonders of nature ever brought to light, representing an ode to eternal splendor," the press release reads. "Interpreted rather than just emulated, nature delivers an endless territory of imagination that only human mastery and Bulgari's aesthetic sense can sublime into true works of art."

Hathaway debuted one of the jewelry pieces, a rare 107,15-carat cushion-cut sapphire dubbed the Mediterranean Reverie necklace, while at Cannes.

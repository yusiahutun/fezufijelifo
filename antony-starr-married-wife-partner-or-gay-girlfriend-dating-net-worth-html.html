Know About Antony's Net Worth

The talented New Zealand actor enjoys a significant net worth of $2 million from his professional career in acting.

Antony, who is best known for his dual role as twins Jethro and Van West in New Zealand's hit comedy/drama Outrageous Fortune, started his professional acting career early in the '90s with guest roles Xena: Warrior Princess and Hercules: The Legendary Journeys and Shortland Street.

For his role in Outrageous Fortunes, he has been awarded the 2007 Air New Zealand Screen Award for Performance by an Actor, Best Actor at the Asian T.V..V. Awards, and the Best Actor at the Qantas Television Awards. Moreover, he was awarded the Best Actor at the inaugural Qantas T.V..V. Awards in 2005.

In 2001, he guest appeared in the T.V..V. series Mercy Peaks Todd Van der Velter for three seasons. He was cast in the soap opera Shortland Street as the brother of Waverley Wilson. Since then, he has had core cast roles in series like Hard Out (Kids' series), guest roles in Street Legal (Drama), and P.E.T. Detectives, and a brief appearance in Serial Killers (a comedy series). 

In 2006, he appeared in the movie No.2. His other film credit includes The World's Fastest Indian, Without a Paddie, and In My Father's Den. 

Starr also played in an Australian film named "Wish You Were Here" in 2012, which bagged him both the Critics Circle of Australia (FCCA) Award and an Australian Film Institute (AACTA) Award. Recently the guy is appeared in the T.V..V. series "American Gothic" as Garrett Hawthorne. He also came out with the leading role as ex-con Lucas Hood in Cinemax's Banshee series.

14 Best Fall Sweaters 2023, Tested &amp; Reviewed by Bazaar Editors

Best Boatneck Sweater

Mango Boat-Neck Knitted Sweater

Best Boatneck Sweater

Mango Boat-Neck Knitted Sweater

Credit: Mango

"This season, I am all about clean lines and sleek silhouettes, and this cozy knit piece from Mango checks every box. In particular, I love its seamless boat-neck detail, which reminds me of something plucked straight out of the wardrobes of Meghan Markle or Audrey Hepburn. It’s easy to pair with existing staples in my closet, too, whether that means styling it with straight-leg jeans and ballet flats or a slinky midi skirt with heeled boots." —Chelsey Sanchez, associate editor

Best Argyle Sweater

Maison Margiela Diamond Pattern Distressed Sweater

Best Argyle Sweater

Maison Margiela Diamond Pattern Distressed Sweater

"Everything looks better when you can tell it's been worn a lot, and I feel like that is especially true with sweaters. Normally I prefer items that that have become distressed because of the life you live in them—yet I haven't been able to stop thinking about this Maison Margiela sweater that looks like it's been through it...even though I know it hasn't. Argyle is something that often feels stuffy but I love how punk it looks when all of the yarn is coming apart and dangling from the raw edges." —Tara Gonzalez, senior fashion editor

Martine Rose Red Oversize Sweater

Best Sweater in a Trending Shade

Martine Rose Red Oversize Sweater

Credit: SSENSE

"I’ve been on the hunt for a bright red sweater and just came across this perfectly oversized one from Martine Rose, who I’m a big fan of. It’s great for dressing up or down, and just the right fit and shade." —Brooke Bobb, fashion news director

Best Striped Sweater

La Ligne Mini Toujours Striped Cashmere Sweater

Best Striped Sweater

La Ligne Mini Toujours Striped Cashmere Sweater

Credit: Net-a-Porter

"La Ligne is the first and last word in striped sweaters. I wanted to add this crewneck to my collection the minute I saw its camel and black combination—and I was glad I did when I pulled it on the first time. The cashmere blend is deliciously soft. I'll wear it with everything from relaxed jeans and comfortable loafers to a terracota slip skirt and knee-high boots." —Halie LeSavage, fashion commerce editor

Best Fair Isle Sweater

Dôen Fran Sweater

Best Fair Isle Sweater

Dôen Fran Sweater

Credit: Dôen

"Heading into fall, I can’t wait to slip into this soft fair isle knit from Doen. The beautiful warm autumn tones embrace the coziness of the English countryside and make it easy to layer under a classic trench coat when it's time to ease into winter. Plus, I love the versatility of a crewneck that gives way to layering over a crisp Oxford shirt, or adorning with layers of delicate gold necklaces." —Jennifer Jenkins, assistant accessories editor

Best Versatile Sweater

LouLou Studio Bruzzi Wool-Cashmere Raglan-Sleeve Crop Sweater

Best Versatile Sweater

LouLou Studio Bruzzi Wool-Cashmere Raglan-Sleeve Crop Sweater

Credit: Bergdorf Goodman

"I love this LouLou studio cropped wool-cashmere sweater. It’s the perfect gray crewneck. I'll wear it all of fall, whether with a high-waisted midi pencil skirt and pointed toe kitten heels, or with mid-rise vintage Levi’s and Mary Jane flats. It’s perfect for layering a striped button-down underneath with the sleeves rolled up. Or, at the office, it can be tied around your neck with a T-shirt and trousers. The options really are endless!" —Jaclyn Alexandra Cohen, senior fashion and accessories editor

Best Button-Up Cardigan

Alex Mill Emma Cardigan

Best Button-Up Cardigan

Alex Mill Emma Cardigan

Credit: Alex Mill

"Alex Mill sweaters are sprinkled all over my apartment: one on my desk chair to battle my office's draft, one on the hook by the door for walking my dog on chilly mornings, one folded in my laptop tote to take to work. Their knits come in timeless colors that I can mix with anything in my wardrobe, and they don't pill or unravel even after I've worn them dozens of times. This button-up cardigan is my favorite for fall 2023. It's knit from a luxuriously warm lambswool—I'm cozy only wearing a ribbed tank top underneath." —Halie LeSavage, fashion commerce editor

Best Colorful Sweater

Mango Polo Neck Sweater

Best Colorful Sweater

Mango Polo Neck Sweater

"I'm usually the type of person who lives in black during colder months, but there's something about a bright, cozy sweater that gets my attention every year. This color-blocked one from Mango definitely fits the bill—and since it nicely features a versatile, medium knit weight, I don't have to worry about overheating. My office mates should expect to see me pairing it this season with tailored trousers, straight leg jeans, and lots of sturdy boots." —Tiffany Dodson, associate beauty commerce editor

Best Black Turtleneck Sweater

Best Black Turtleneck Sweater

ANINE BING Sydney Sweater

Credit: Anine Bing

"The bigger, the better is my personal mantra when it comes to the perfect fall sweater. Last season, I basically lived in this camel brown crewneck number from Anine Bing and I'm looking to continue that streak with the brand's black Sydney sweater. I'm already dreaming of pairing it with stirrup leggings and chunky moto boots or wide leg jeans and my favorite platform Ugg mules. If you see me wearing it weekly, mind your business!" —Bianca Betancourt, culture editor

Best Polo Sweater

Zegna x The Elder Statesman Cashmere and Wool Polo Sweater

Best Hallmark Holiday Movies to Watch

1

Round and Round

@2023 Hallmark Media

Drawing inspiration from Groundhog Day, Round and Round takes place at a Hanukkah party that Rachel (Vic Michaelis) can't escape from. As she gets closer to Zach (One Tree Hill's Bryan Greenberg), the cute guy her grandma wants to set her up with, Rachel has to figure out how to stop living the same day over and over again.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

2

Navigating Christmas

@2023 Hallmark Media

Following her divorce, Melanie decides to spend the holiday season on a remote island, along with her son Jason. Rather than being the idyllic retreat she hoped for, Melanie becomes responsible for the local lighthouse, which brings her into close proximity of its cantankerous owner, Peter.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

3

Christmas with a Kiss

©2023 Hallmark Media

In true Hallmark fashion, Christmas with a Kiss follows a woman who heads home for the holiday season to help her family plan their annual Christmas Carnival. There, she unexpectedly reunites with someone from her past. Will sparks fly this Christmas?

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

The Christmas House

©2023 Hallmark Media5

Sealed with a List

@2023 Hallmark Media

Realizing that she hasn't accomplished any of her New Year's resolutions, Carley decides to spend the 31 days of December making changes. She enlists the help of her ex-boss' hapless son, Wyatt, who's facing his own crisis of faith. The unlikely pair works together to end the year on a positive note.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

6

A Kiss Before Christmas

©2023 Hallmark Media

A Kiss Before Christmas reunites Desperate Housewives alums Teri Hatcher and James Denton for some holiday magic. After wishing that his life had turned out differently, Ethan awakes to find himself in another timeline, in which he's neither a father nor a husband. In order to get his life back, Ethan has to convince his wife Joyce that his wild story is true, and that his wish was a terrible mistake.

7

Holiday Hotline

@2023 Hallmark Media

Over the holiday season, Abby takes a job giving advice on the "holiday hotline," for anyone struggling with their cooking during Thanksgiving and Christmas. She strikes up a conversation with widower John, with whom she feels a connection. But in real life, Abby has fallen for Jack, not realizing that they're the same man.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

8

Christmas in Harmony

©2023 Hallmark Media9

The Santa Summit

@2023 Hallmark Media

Jordin, Ava, and Stella attend the Santa Summit, at which attendees dress up in their finest Santa outfits and take part in festive events. During the night, Jordin meets Liam (Virgin River's Benjamin Hollingsworth), an interesting guy who is also dressed as Saint Nick. But will they find one another again before the Santa Summit ends?

10

Inventing the Christmas Prince

©2023 Hallmark Media

Shelby (Tamera Mowry-Housley) is planning on quitting her job, when her daughter Grace has an interesting revelation. For some reason, Grace believes that her mom's boss Evan, who does a fantastic Scrooge impression, is actually a character known as the "Christmas Prince."

WATCH ON PRIME VIDEO

11

Christmas Island

@2023 Hallmark Media

Kate's dream job as a private pilot is thrown into jeopardy when a snowstorm forces her to land on Christmas Island. In order to get her clients to Switzerland for the holiday season, Kate must work with local air traffic controller Oliver, while also getting involved in some festivities.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

12

A Gingerbread Romance

©2023 Hallmark Media

Tia Mowry stars as Taylor, an architect who is entered into a ginger bread house competition. After fighting with her original pastry chef, Taylor finds baker Adam, a single dad who reluctantly agrees to take part. While working together, Taylor starts to understand what it means to be part of a family at Christmas.

13

Time for Her to Come Home for Christmas

©2023 Hallmark Media

In a move sure to delight teen TV fans, Time for Her to Come Home for Christmas unites 90210's Shenae Grimes-Beech and The O.C.'s Chris Carmack for a holiday romance. After the death of her mom, Carly agrees to lead a small town choir over the holidays. There, she meets Matthew, a veteran who has recently returned home.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

14

A Merry Scottish Christmas

©2023 Hallmark Media

A Merry Scottish Christmas reunites Party of Five siblings Lacey Chabert and Scott Wolf. Lindsey and Brad are somewhat estranged when they agree to travel to their mother's childhood home in Scotland for the holidays. While getting acquainted with the beautiful town, the siblings discover a secret that's tormented their mom for decades.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

15

Holiday Heritage

©2023 Hallmark Media

Ella decides to repair bad family relations over the holiday season. After reuniting with her ex-boyfriend Griffin, Ella encourages her disparate family members to celebrate both Christmas and Kwanzaa, and to find a new way to move forward as a family unit.

16

Rescuing Christmas

©2023 Hallmark Media

Photographer Erin (Rachael Leigh Cook) wishes that Christmas never existed, not realizing that two of Santa's elves are on hand to grant the wish. Instantly regretting her decision, Erin and her potential love interest Sam (Sam Page) work to reintroduce the joy of the holiday season to Erin's family.

WATCH ON HALLMARK MOVIES NOW

17

Christmas in Notting Hill

©2023 Hallmark Media

American Georgia travels to England to spend the holidays in Notting Hill with her sister Lizzie. A chance encounter with English soccer player Graham makes Georgia wonder if she's found romance. Plus, Graham is particularly smitten with Georgia because she has no idea who he is. But can the unlikely couple turn their spark into a relationship?

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

18

Holiday Road

@2023 Hallmark Media

After a group of strangers finds themselves stranded at the airport over the holidays, they decide to hire a van and travel to Denver together. The trip is far from simple, however, and the unexpected group is forced to work together to make the best of their circumstances.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

19

My Christmas Guide

©2023 Hallmark Media

College professor Trevor loses his eyesight and decides to adopt a guide dog. During the process, he spends time with his dog's trainer, Peyton, with whom he develops a real connection. As Christmas approaches, Trevor realizes that he might be falling in love for the first time in a long time.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

20

Heaven Down Here

©2023 Hallmark Media

After a snowstorm hits a small town on Christmas Eve, four strangers find themselves sheltering together at a local diner. Throughout the night, the group shares their woes, bonds over unexpected commonalities, and provides much needed support to one another.

WATCH ON HALLMARK TV

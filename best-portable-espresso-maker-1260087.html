    Choosing the best portable battery-operated or manual expresso machine can be quite a task! Hence, after carefully analyzing all the features of all pocket espresso machines, we have listed down a few factors you can keep in mind while making your next purchase.

    How to Choose the Best Portable Espresso Maker?

    Here are a few factors that you can consider before purchasing a portable expresso maker:

    1. Material and Design: While looking for a portable espresso machine that can serve as an essential travel accessory it is important to ascertain that the material is made up of break, jerk, and scratch-resistant materials as it may tamper the aesthetic appeal of the product and also effect its working efficiency. To add on, it should have a built-in paper filter and a built-in ergonomically designed manual grinder to facilitate easy grinding of coffee beans without much effort and pain.  Also, it should not contain any PFAs and Teflon as it may trigger inert allergies and also cause digestion issues. 

    2. Pressure Bar: For a perfectly aromatic, rich, and creamy cup of coffee, it is important to get a coffee machine that has a pressure bar of 15 bars and above so that the espresso-making process is an effortless one. 

    3. Weight and Dimensions: Look for products with lightweight construction and a compact design that are easy to carry, store, and transport. 

    4. Additional Accessories: To enjoy a coffee without being hindered by routine maintenance issues, it is important to look for a coffee machine with additional strainers, funnels, and plungers so that it is easy to clean and replace, and use over time. 

    5. Easy to Clean: Lastly the coffee machine should be dishwasher friendly and have an easy cleaning procedure without the need for abrasive scrubbing and polishing.

    FAQ's

    How does a portable espresso maker work?

    Portable expresso machines make use of a piston that forces water under pressure over the ground coffee.

    How do I clean a portable espresso maker?

    Remove all the washable parts from the coffee-making machine and wash it under running water. You can also check if your portable coffee machine is dishwasher-friendly or not.

    Can I use a portable espresso maker for regular coffee?

    A portable expresso machine can be used to make regular coffee and is more convenient due to its lightweight construction and ergonomic design. However, you may need a milk frother separately if it is not included in the design of your espresso maker.

    Do portable espresso makers require batteries or electricity?

    Most portable coffee makers have an in-built rechargeable battery system that makes it suitable to have coffee while on the go without the need for frequent charging. Some designs involve wholly manual operation, such as the WACACO Picopresso Portable Espresso Maker and the Flair Signature Manual Espresso Maker.

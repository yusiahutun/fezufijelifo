Billie Eilish strips completely topless to finally unveil enormous hidden tattoo

Billie Eilish has left her fans in a total frenzy after sharing long awaited pictures of her new back tattoo.

The singer stripped totally topless as she laid down on her tattoo artist’s table and prepared to get inked. Billie wore a pair of black tracksuit bottoms as she perched up on her elbows and showed off the intricate design, which ran from the bottom of her neck to the bottom of her spine.

Eilish was lit up in red while the stencil laid next to her on the bed. She wore her hair up to keep it out of the way and turned her head to the side as someone captured a picture of the moment.

In the caption, Billie added a scissor emoji as fans rushed to share their thoughts on her rather unique new ink. Someone branded the tattoo “fye,” a slang term meaning “amazing,” while others lost their minds over Eilish’s “tattoo reveal”.

Someone else wrote: “Without words, this is incredible [flaming heart emojis],” as another fan branded her “so hot”.

A third person begged Eilish to “show them the full version” of her tattoo while other fans chimed in that they “so badly want to know the story behind it”. They speculated that the pattern “could have a really interesting story or meaning behind it”.

Followers gathered to decipher Billie’s ink, with one person noting that the pattern “looked like the grain in wood” and another said it resembled “Venom’s symbiotic material”.

Billie loves to post on Instagram and recently shared a quick clip of herself posing with her tongue out. The Bad Guy singer wore a low cut black vest top as she held the camera high above her head and scrunched up her face.

Eilish accessorised with a chunky silver chain round her neck which featured skull charms to link the piece together.

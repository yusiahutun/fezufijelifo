Lindsay Lohan shopped for a new jacket yesterday but clearly should have begun her spree in the lingerie section.

But it seems unlikely the girl who is photographed everywhere she goes was entirely oblivious to the problem.

She memorably teased fans posing in a provocative Marilyn-style shoot and here again she seemed to know exactly what she was doing.

Although the effect of gravity didn't always show her figure in the best light.

Bit of bling: Lindsay tried on a variety of 80s-style embellished jackets

Just days after narrowly avoiding jail for breaking the terms of her drink-driving ban, Lindsay had reason to be in good spirits as she tried on various 80s -style embellished jackets.

As part of her three-year probation for driving under the influence of cocaine and alcohol, Lindsay had been ordered to attend a substance abuse programme.

But she had missed several classes and was warned by judge Marsha Revel to 'get serious' about her recovery.

Hanging loose: Lindsay reaches into her bag

Luckily for Lindsay, Judge Revel accepted her excuses that she had been too busy filming to attend classes.

As she shopped yesterday, reports emerged in the U.S. that four women had been arrested on suspicion of breaking into her home last August.

At least two of those arrested were allegedly involved in the burglaries of Orlando Bloom and The O.C star Rachel Bilson's homes as well.

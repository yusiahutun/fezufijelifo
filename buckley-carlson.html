Early Life

Buckley Carlson was born in 1997 in Virginia and the names of his parents are Tucker Carlson and Susan Andrews. Buckley Carlson Career

As he received a degree in political science, he decided to build his career in the field of politics. In June 2017 he worked as a communication intern at the White House for Donald John Trump. Later in 2019, he was seen working for Rep Jim Banks as Staff Assistant and Special assistant. In January 2020 he worked as press secretary for Representative Jim Banks. Next year he was seen in the House Republican Study Committee of Representative Jim Banks as Deputy Communications Director. Currently, in 2022 he is working as Communication Director for Representative Jim Banks. He will be seen working in various other positions in the future and even for other representatives.

Who is Buddy Valastro?

Bartolo “Buddy” Valastro Jr. Was born on March 3, 1977, in Hoboken, New Jersey, USA. He is the son of Mary Valastro and Buddy Valastro senior.

Buddy was raised alongside four sisters and attended Ridgefield Park High School. Additionally, he took baking classes from the Teterboro Campus of Bergen County Technical High School.

At the age of 11, Buddy took his first step in the culinary world by working for his father, who, at the time, was the head baker at his family-owned Carlo’s Bakery.

Buddy Valastro

The established was opened in 1910 and later bought up by Buddy Sr more than 50-years later. After his father’s sudden death in the mid-90s, Buddy took over the business, leading it to great and new heights.

His cake designs were appreciated by soon-to-be-wed couples who lined outside his store in droves. Buddy’s cakes even appeared on the front page of reputable magazines.

In 2007, his fame reached new heights as he stepped up to work for the Food Network Challenge.

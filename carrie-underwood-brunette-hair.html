Carrie Underwood Debuts Brunette-ish Hair Style

Carrie Underwood has always been for her long, lean legs and her lavish blonde locks. But now, she's changing it up, with a new hair color.

The 'Blown Away' singer debuted her new hair look on the red carpet at the 2014 Billboard Music Awards, where she performed 'Somethin' Bad' with fellow country star Miranda Lambert.

“It’s just a little bit darker,” Underwood tells People.  “I don’t think I’d want to or could even pull off being a brunette, but it’s just nice sometimes to change it up a bit."

"I’ve been super blonde forever, and I feel like there are a lot of super-blonde ladies in music so I thought I’d darken it just a little bit.”

Her hair is still long as ever, but instead of being overall blonde, the songstress darkened up her look just a bit, going a few shades more toward brunette, but still being blonde enough to be extremely recognizable as Underwood.

She makes sure to take care of her tresses, no matter what color they are, and dishes, “Sitting in the tub with a hair mask on is boring. I just put conditioner on in the shower, do everything else, and then rinse it out.”

Fans will get another look at the country star and her gal pal Lambert at the 2014 CMT Awards where they will be again lighting up the stage with 'Somethin' Bad.'

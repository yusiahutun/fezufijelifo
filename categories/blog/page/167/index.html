Michigan International Speedway Launches New Festival Series

published on July 12, 2024
BROOKLYN, MI (CelebrityAccess) — The operators of the Michigan International Speedway have announced that they are launching a new festival series Kelorah to coincide with NASCAR race weekends at the track. Keloorah is two days of post-race live music and activities on Fridays and Saturdays during the June and August NASCAR weekends at MIS. The festivities will include live concerts, deejays, a go-kart experience a mobile video game arcade, an outdoor sports bar, tailgate games, foam parties and paint parties framed around NASCAR events on the MIS property.

PARIS (CelebrityAccess) — Kendrick Lamar, Rosalía and Stray Kids have been announced as the headliners for the 2023 return of Lollapalooza Paris. Now in its 5th year, the festival will take place from July 21-23 at the Hippodrome De Longchamp, a 140 acre horse racetrack located on the Route des Tribunes at the Bois de Boulogne in Paris, France. The fest will feature four full stages of music with a lineup that also includes Lil Nas X, Aya Nakamura, Kygo, Central Cee, OneRepublic, Lindsey Stirling, Niall Horan, Rezz and many more.

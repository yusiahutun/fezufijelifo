Source: En.wikipedia.org Zafer Bayram?, also known as Victory Day, is one of the most significant national holidays celebrated in Turkey. This day commemorates the decisive Turkish victory in the Battle of Dumlup?nar in 1922, which marked the end of the Turkish War of Independence. Every year, on August 30th, Turks gather to honor the valor and sacrifices made by their ancestors during this historic event.

Nicole Kidman, Jennifer Lopez, and More Rely on These Haircare Picks

You can always count on celebrities to serve when it comes to hairstyles. Whether dyeing it an eye-catching shade or doing a complete chop, A-listers seem to have a new hairstyle every month. And like always, we’re surprised that their hair still looks so good. Luckily, a few celebrities have spilled their secrets for maintaining luscious locks. Now, you can score some of their favorite hair care products for less — and here’s how. We’ve found a handful of value sets that include products used by stars like Nicole Kidman, Jennifer Lopez, Kim Kardashian, and more. Even better, they come from shopper-loved brands such as Olaplex, Vegamour, and Nioxin. From improving everything from thinning and split ends, these celeb-backed products are worth adding to your cart immediately if you want to revive your hair.

Looking to have a gorgeous blow-out like J.Lo? Color Wow’s Dream Smooth Travel Kit includes Dream Coat, a frizz-fighting spray that celebrity hair stylist Chris Appleton uses on Jennifer Lopez. This internet-famous product leaves you with a sleek, healthy look for days, per a reviewer. It repels humidity and protects against heat that causes breakage. Along with this product, the value set also comes with a shampoo and conditioner helps make hair soft and manageable.  

If thinning, breaking hair is a big concern for you, try the Vegamour Gro Revitalizing Shampoo and Conditioner Kit. Nicole Kidman said that the brand’s products take years of damage off of her locks. The Gro shampoo, conditioner, and hair serum saved her weakened strands from years of straightening, coloring, and other treatments for acting gigs. And we totally get why — the shampoo and conditioner use plant-based ingredients to strengthen, promote thicker-looking strands, and restore shine. Luckily, you can get both for sightlessly less in this $86 kit at Vegamour. 

Another solid choice for thinning hair is Nioxin System Kit 4. It features a color-safe shampoo, conditioner, scalp treatment, and thickening spray that helps revitalize hair from root to tip. In addition, it promises to restore strands as well as reduce shedding — and it’s Alyssa Milano’s hair care brand of choice. Milano previously shared on Twitter that Nioxin’s shampoo and conditioner “tremendously” helped her with hair loss caused by COVID-19. And right now, the set is just $51. 

For those with dry or frizz-prone hair, you’ll want Verb’s Ghost Oil in your hair care collection. It’s incredibly versatile for all hair types including straight, wavy, curly, and coily. The lightweight oil adds softness and shine, controls frizz, and smooths flyaways all in one. Celebrity hair stylist Jennifer Yepez previously told InStyle that she used the Ghost Oil on Salma Hayek’s hair for the 95th Academy Awards earlier this year. She added, “it hydrated and gave the perfect texture that I wanted for her rock-and-roll cool-girl hair.” 

Countless shoppers and celebrities alike swear by Olaplex products for saving their damaged hair. Drew Barrymore and Kim Kardashian both keep Olaplex No.3 Hair Perfector in their routine, which helps to reduce breakage and split ends. Kardashian even credits it for keeping her hair healthy when she went blonde. But if you really want to give your hair a boost, check out this Olaplex set that’s marked down to $55. It also comes with the No.4 Bond Maintenance Shampoo and No. 5 Bond Maintenance Conditioner that are key to adding strength and moisture back to your tresses.

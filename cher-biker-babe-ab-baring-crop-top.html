Cher looks unreal in ab-baring leather waistcoat and retro satin pants

The 76-year-old Oscar-winning actress looked incredible in her red carpet getup

    Cher, 76, was amongst the stars at the Los Angeles premiere of on Sunday night.

    The Hollywood star's sartorial flair has long been defined by belly-baring tops and funky flares, with her archive of leather jackets and fringed vests synonymous with her emergence to fame in the sixties. On Monday, her style once again reigned supreme.

    The hitmaker led the star-studded arrivals in an ultra-flattering outfit consisting of a satin puffer jacket emblazoned with a striking oval emblem and ab-baring wide-leg trousers that highlighted her feminine frame. 

    Channelling a biker babe aesthetic, the legendary songstress appeared to wear a plunging leather waistcoat beneath her chic outerwear, levelling up her look with black platform heels. 

    Cher's raven hair was styled in voluminous curls as she rocked a vampy makeup look that highlighted her ageless beauty glow. The singer-turned-actress, who is believed to be engaged to her 37-year-old beau Alexander Edwards, was recently forced to slash $10million off the asking price of her Malibu mansion. 

    The star's plans to move emerged just weeks after she left fans confused over her 'proposal' photo shared on Twitter. Cher took to the social media app in December last year to share a photo of a dazzling pear-cut diamond set between a glittering band encrusted with sparkling diamonds. 

    The jewel, displayed in a plush black jewellery box, left the songstress entirely speechless as she penned: "THERE R NO WORDS", followed by the name of her beau, "ALEXANDER, A.E." 

    Fans swiftly flooded the thread of her post with messages of congratulations, though several were left confused by her cryptic tweet. "Did he propose?" asked a fan, while another said: "Did he put a ring on it?" 

    Cher has not yet revealed whether or not her cryptic post was confirmation of Alexander's proposal.

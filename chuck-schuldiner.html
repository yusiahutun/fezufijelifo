Biography

Chuck Schuldiner was born in Glen Cove, NY on May 13, 1967. Guitarist, singer, songwriter, and death metal pioneer with the metal group Death, the band known for the debut album Scream Bloody Gore. Schuldiner paved the way for extreme metal musicians like Anders Frieden and his group Flames. He picked up the guitar as a way to cope with his brother’s untimely death. He drew inspiration from artists like Billy Idol and KISS.

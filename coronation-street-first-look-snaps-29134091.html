Coronation Street first-look snaps at Daisy and Daniel's wedding as major star 'missing'

Daniel is all set to tie the knot - but where is the bride?

Daisy and Daniel’s big day has arrived on the cobbles, but will the white wedding go as planned?

These exclusive Coronation Street snaps show Daniel waiting for his fiancée with a worried look on his face, as guests including Jenny Connor (Sally Ann Matthews) and Sarah Barlow (Tina O’Brien) wait.

Will he eventually get his own way and stop the wedding by any means necessary?

Daisy should be enjoying the happiest time of her life as she plans her upcoming wedding to fiancé Daniel Osbourne (Rob Mallard).

However, after a brief meeting with Justin when she was in a hospital waiting room some weeks ago, she has been constantly harassed on her social media.

Justin has sent her several bunches of flowers, as well as leaving her an endless stream of messages online.

When Justin made himself known to her in the Rovers, Daisy was baffled as she didn't recognise him but he continued to send the messages, believing that he and Daisy are in love.

She repeatedly blocked and deleted Justin's posts, but each time, he created a new account and got back in touch.

Fans will have to wait and see if Daisy and Daniel make it down the aisle.

It comes after Corrie boss Iain MacLeod teased that Daisy will be facing "a certain degree of danger" ahead of her nuptials with Daniel.

He explained: "It's a kind of story about being a young woman being on the internet and the kind of dangers that face influencers.

"Daisy is quite small fry but she's got a certain amount of online following and this essentially brings her a certain degree of danger as the wedding approaches.

"There's loads of good fun to be had there but in the background there's this creeping danger that arises from Daisy's online life and by the time that surfaces, it takes a while for Daisy and Daniel to realise that this danger is brewing.

"It's a hugely important story for us and one you don't generally see on television.

Coronation Street airs Monday, Wednesday and Friday on ITV1 at 8pm

If you’ve never used an advent calendar before, here’s how it works: Each calendar has a certain number of compartments that fans can open up as they count down the days to Halloween. Inside the compartments are festive surprises like ornaments, mini books, puzzles, and other holiday-themed trinkets. Advent calendars, first used by German Lutherans in the 19th and 20th centuries, have long been associated with Christmas, but as the excitement for the end of the year seemingly gets earlier and earlier, these spooky-themed calendars offer a new way to enjoy the holiday.

Do Barbiecore in royal fashion like Catherine, Princess of Wales Greta Gerwigs film made pink

Greta Gerwig’s Barbie movie, starring Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling, sparked a passion for all-pink looks even before it hit cinemas in July. For a day of engagements about London in May, the royal style icon chose a pastel pink pantsuit from British label Alexander McQueen. She accessorised the smartly tailored look with a coordinating blouse and an embellished belt.

With these pieces, you can look every bit as chic. So come Princess Barbie, let’s go party …

Sharon Osbourne vs. Lily Collins!

What do Sharon Osbourne and Lily Collins have in common? Apparently, their style!

On Wednesday, Sharon Osbourne hit the red carpet for the "America's Got Talent" finale in a very elaborate gown … one we've definitely seen before!

Osbourne donned Monique Lhuillier's three-quarter sleeve tulle and lace gown for the event. The 59-year-old paired the cobalt blue embroidered ensemble with sparkly teardrop earrings and red nails (matching her signature short red hair!).

Maybe she's channeling her inner starlet, because "Mirror, Mirror" star, Lily Collins, wore the same dress back in June to the Vanity Fair Oscar Party!

The 23-year-old also matched her dress with teardrop earrings, but she opted for an elegant updo to complete her look.

While both of the ladies look very sophisticated in the lace gown, we have to give this one to Lily! Collins really does look like a prinncess in the dress, and the nude color is perfection against her skin.

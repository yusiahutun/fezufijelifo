Elizabeth Taylor's Beverly Hills mansion from 1950s goes on sale

One of Elizabeth Taylor's former mansions is hitting the market for the first time in 21 years.

Coldwell Banker Global Luxury released a statement to DailyMail.com on Tuesday that the property has been listed for $15.9 million. 

The stunning dwelling, which boasts a large swimming pool and scenic views of the Pacific Ocean, is nestled up in the hills and is gated, offering maximum security.

Hollywood legend Taylor lived in the mansion in the 1950s during her marriage to British film star Michael Wilding who worked with Joan Crawford in the 1953 hit movie Torch Song.

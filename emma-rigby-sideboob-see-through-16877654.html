Emma Rigby serves up serious sideboob in raunchy lace outfit

The former Hollyoaks babe turned her back on UK TV to join some of tinsel town’s finest in Hollywood.

But blonde bombshell Emma Rigby decided to grace us all with her jaw-dropping beauty this week as she took to the red carpet at the world premiere of The Festival at the Cineworld Leicester Square.

And we can’t thank her enough.

Donning a seriously sexy plunging floor-length gown, the 28-year-old actress ensured all eyes were on her as she showcased her fabulous figure.

The star's show-stopping ensemble featured sheer lace cut-out detail, ensuring she flashed plenty of sideboob along with a hint of her lithe legs, thanks to a sly split at the front of the frock.

Meanwhile Emma – who has bagged herself a minor role in the new film – styled her golden locks into glamorous pin-up girl waves, which she swept to one side, framing her pretty face.

Adding extra sex appeal to the provocative look, the Prisoners’ Wives star opted for a smokey eye and a pop of bright red lipstick.

She completed the sophisticated getup with a pair of sparkly strappy heels.

Emma has carved out quite the successful career in recent years, but it wasn’t always easy.

Speaking to The Sun, the starlet revealed how she had been turned down from several interviews following her exit from Hollyoaks.

She told the publication: “When I left Hollyoaks I could barely get any interviews for months. Casting directors wouldn’t let me in the door.

“They would just see ‘Hollyoaks’ and say no.

“So when I came to America, it was incredibly liberating to be given the chance to prove what I could do and that I could transform for roles.”

But, it seems that things have turned out better than ever for the stunning star, who now counts some of Hollywood’s finest amongst her close pals.

“Now I often have to pinch myself when I think about the things I’m doing,” she told the paper.

“I’ve become good friends with Ben Kingsley, and when we went for dinner the other day he dropped into his character from Sexy Beast.

“He was also telling me stories about Martin Scorcese, who is my favourite director ever, and I was sitting there thinking: ‘I can’t believe this.’”

End Grain Walnut Butcher Block Laurel Mercantile

  • Made in Laurel, Mississippi
  • American hardwood
  • Oil finished & food safe
  • Pairs well with Butcher Block Care products
  • Three sizes available:
    • Small - 9″ x 12″ x 1.5″ 
    • Medium - 11.5″ x 15″ x 1.5″ 
    • Large - 14.75″ x 19.75″ x 1.5″ 

Walnut wood is a luxury wood used to make high-end furniture, instruments like guitars and violins, and even kitchen accessories like butcher blocks. It's hard, heavy, and resilient, capable of resisting warping. Read more about the difference in wood types here. 

Never put your butcher block in the dishwasher. The best way to care for these heirloom pieces is with simple soap and water after use. You can find full care instructions here.

Due to the nature of wood products, not every butcher block will look the same in color, grain pattern, and branding. Each piece is unique and we think that makes them even more special.

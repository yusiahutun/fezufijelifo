Fat Transfer To Breasts - Liposuction Fat Transfer Boob Job

Though it doesn't have a snappy name like a Brazilian Butt Lift, a fat transfer to breasts is essentially the same thing. Consider it the implant-less boob job: a plastic surgeon liposuctions fat from your body then re-injects it back into your breast tissue, creating fuller breasts and cleavage.

We followed one woman to the office Elite Body Sculpture, where Dr. Aaron Rollins would use his Airsculpt technique to build fuller breasts. Not only is the patient awake during the entire procedure, but they can get up and walk away with minimal downtime. In just three weeks, the surviving fat tissue from the transfer is settled—and the results are permanent. Watch the entire surgery and recovery process above.

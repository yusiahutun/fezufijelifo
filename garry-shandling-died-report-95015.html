RIPComedian Garry Shandling Has Died at Age 66 (REPORT)

Rest in peace, Garry Shandling.

The actor and comedian sadly died on Thursday, March 24 in LA from unknown causes, TMZ reported. He was 66-years-old.

A source close to Garry claims he was healthy and talkative the morning of his sudden death. Kathy Griffin additionally shared a photo of herself alongside the late actor and Bob Odenkirk on Twitter on Tuesday, March, 20.

Garry in 1980.

Throughout his showbiz career, Garry was most known for his performance on It’s Garry Shandling’s Show and _ The Larry Sanders Show_. He additionally wrote an episode of the hit 1970s show, _ Welcome Back, Kotter_.

Gigi Hadid Wore the Littlest Lacy Black Dress Underneath a Gigantic Leather Jacket

Gigi Hadid put her own personal twist on a wardrobe staple.

After walking the runway for Miu Miu’s Spring/Summer 2024 show, Hadid turned up at the brand’s after-party in a jaw-dropping all-black outfit.

The supermodel wore a lacy little black dress featuring a glittering sequined floral pattern, a turtleneck, see-through mesh fabric, a black ribbon belt, and a ruched black satin hemline. She paired the gorgeous piece with sheer black tights and patent leather pump slingbacks with a sensible kitten heel and multiple buckle straps. To accessorize, she added a small black leather Miu Miu purse, pearl drop earrings by Mejuri, and an array of plain rings.

Victor Boyko//Getty Images

Arnold Jerocki//Getty Images

Outside the venue, Hadid upped the ante with her choice of outerwear: an oversize black leather jacket with a collar and a zip-up front. She added a pair of narrow rectangular sunglasses, also in black.

Among the other A-listers at Miu Miu’s after-party were Adriana Lima, Paris Hilton, Troye Sivan, Sydney Sweeney, and Emma Corrin.

MEGA//Getty Images

Earlier in the day, Hadid offered a glimpse of her supermodel off-duty style. She looked casually cool in a ribbed white crop top layered underneath a dark brown leather jacket from Mango with an oversize fit. For her bottoms, she wore a long black maxi skirt. She finished off the look with black ballet flats with a tiny bow adorned at the toe, a black Yankees baseball cap, a black purse with gold hardware, and a pair of small oval sunglasses with a gold rim.

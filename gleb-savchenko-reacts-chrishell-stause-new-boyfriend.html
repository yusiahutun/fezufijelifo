In response, 37-year-old Gleb issued a statement to HELLO!, in which he also spoke about the speculation surrounding his relationship with Chrishell.

"Elena and I have had longstanding issues in our marriage. This has been an ongoing situation between Elena and I paired with poor timing. It was my deepest hope to keep our private life just that: private. I am saddened and disappointed to see that Elena has chosen not to do the same."

Chrishell split from husband Justin Hartley one year ago

Chrishell, who divorced actor Justin Hartley one year ago, also released a statement herself, saying: "I am so saddened about the news of Gleb and Elena's split. It is unfortunate that this has caused rumours to swirl about my personal life.

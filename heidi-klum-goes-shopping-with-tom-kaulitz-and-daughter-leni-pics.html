Heidi Klum Goes Shopping With Tom Kaulitz and Daughter Leni: Pics

Sunday funday! Heidi Klum was all smiles as she was spotted shopping at the Rose Bowl Flea Market in Pasadena, California, on October 13. The America’s Got Talent star was accompanied by her husband, Tom Kaulitz, and her eldest kid, 15-year-old daughter Leni, as she was seen perusing the outdoor shopping market for vintage clothing and furniture.

The 46-year-old beauty was dressed super casually for the fun-filled shopping trip. Heidi was supporting her man’s former band as she donned a Tokio Hotel tour T-shirt, black sweatpants underneath a camouflage skirt and a denim shirt wrapped around her waist.

The Germany’s Next Top Model judge accessorized her stylish look with a pair of aviator sunglasses and an Escada handbag. Tom, on the other hand, sported a green shirt and black jeans, while Leni looked super cute in a white crop top and denim pants. What a trendy bunch!

During their outing, Heidi could be seen sipping on a large can of Mexican beer while she strolled around the Rose Bowl Flea Market. The couple’s outing comes just two months after they tied the knot in a romantic Italian ceremony in August.

Prior to their wedding, the iconic supermodel — who actually married Tom in a private ceremony in February — gushed about how excited her kids were to gain an amazing new stepdad. “The kids adore Tom. They can’t wait for the wedding!” the brunette beauty shared with U.K.’s Closer magazine in March. “They’ll be very involved in it all.”

Ever since Heidi and her rocker beau crossed paths on the set of Germany’s Next Top Model in February 2018, the Hollywood favorite has never been happier. In fact, the Germany native gushed about their incredible relationship last September.

“He is the kindest, sweetest, most caring, loving person. I feel so lucky. I don’t know the last time I was this happy in my life,” Heidi — who was previously married to ex-husband Seal from 2005 to 2014, as well as Ric Pipino from 1997 to 2002 — told People at the time. “He is absolutely wonderful. Maybe it is because he is German and we understand each other in a different way. But so far so good.”

Tom and Heidi are serious goals!

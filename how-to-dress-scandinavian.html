How to Dress Scandinavian in 2023 &amp; Be a Fashion Girlie

If you’ve had your eyes on the street style lately, you’re probably wondering how to dress Scandinavian. There’s something so effortlessly chic about how Scandinavian fashion girlies dress. They mix and match basic garments, yet somehow always manage to possess a level of cool that’s rare to find elsewhere. It’s their nonchalant, barely-has-to-try flair that hooks you in and makes you realize you hate everything in your closet and need to pare down your wardrobe.

But what’s the biggest key to perfecting the Scandinavian look? It’s ensuring that your clothing works from season to season and can easily be layered. With this region’s style, you won’t have to pursue new items every year, as the whole ethos really encourages the building of a capsule wardrobe. According to Scandi clothing brand Glúmur, they “embrace the essence of Scandinavian lifestyle and fashion, embodying the sporty yet casual simplicity that defines the region. A well-curated wardrobe is powerful and true style doesn’t need to be reinvented every season; it’s about enhancing what you already have.”

Scandinavian fashion also puts comfort above all else. Locals are all about wearing what’s practical—think: “dad” sneakers, crisp (or poofy) button-down shirts, baggy jeans, and cozy outerwear. You’ll rarely catch Scandi cool girls wearing cropped tops, skinny jeans, or stiletto heels, because, well, they’re both impractical and uncomfortable.

If you ask Glúmur for the most fitting Scandinavian fashion essentials, it’s the brand’s oversized blazers, relaxed tops, and laidback denim. These pieces allow you to seamlessly (and comfortably) transition from day to night and work to leisure whenever you please.

With that said, we’ve gathered six styles we adore from the brand so that you can begin to build your own dream Scandi closet. From pinstripe blazers to wide-leg jeans, here are some Scandinavian fashion essentials that our eyes (and wallets) are glued on right now.

  • Oversized Blazer

    First things first in Scandinavian fashion—get yourself an oversized blazer. The classic tailoring on the fabric makes for a structural, elegant fit. With this jacket, you’ll get double the pockets (there are some on the inside), and did we mention everything is sustainably-sourced? This blazer is made from leftover wool via a French luxury fashion brand.

  • Wide-Leg Jeans

    If you’re pondering over which style of denim can withstand the test of time, we can absolutely guarantee that it’s the wide-leg silhouette. This gorgeous pair is high-waisted, made from organic cotton, and can be styled with a slouchy tee or oversized white dress shirt to twin with all the fashion girlies.

  • Button-Down Shirt

    The Fredrik Shirt is definitely a Scandinavian wardrobe staple, since it’s nearly impossible for it to go out of style. It features a straight silhouette with a point collar, buttoned cuffs, and a curved hem. Wear it loose or tuck in into a pair of baggy jeans.

  • Relaxed Coat

    Made with a blend of sustainably-sourced alpaca wool from Italy and taking on a stunning oversized silhouette, we think we’ve found our absolute dream winter coat. We’re just itching to wear this baby out on the city streets over a pair of jeans.

  • Straight-Leg Jeans

    Whoever said straight-leg jeans were out of style has not seen how the Scandinavians style them. Complete this pair with leather boots and a basic top. Oh, and don’t forget to throw a blazer over top!

  • Oversized Shirt

    For a more avant-garde look that’ll still stay on trend for years to come, opt for the Caia Shirt. Made from an eco-friendly viscose crepe fabric, this top is unique but still casual enough to wear just about anywhere.

How to Style Leather Leggings 7 Different Ways

Incredibly versatile and figure-flattering, leather leggings deserve an A-list spot in your closet. The bonafide rockstar of fabrics, faux leather offers a cruelty-free alternative that delivers all the haute couture without haunting your conscious. 

Rest assured, you don’t have to have moves like Jagger to pull off faux leather. Leggings are an easy way to slide into the slick material, making them a wardrobe must-have. Thanks to their supple coating, leather leggings allow you to look sophisticated while staying comfortable, helping you get away with wearing athleisure to work and any after-hour plans. Their sleek and simple silhouette can also be worn in a myriad of ways, resulting in endless outfit options, no matter what your aesthetic is.

You can lean into your grungier side by pairing the patent pants with a motorcycle jacket and oversized tee. If keeping things prim and proper suits your fancy more, a structured peplum top instantly elevates leather leggings. Meanwhile, a crisp white button-down shirt keeps things business casual. Extra points if you can find leggings in a glossy finish or highly pigmented shades like emerald green and oxblood to liven up your ensemble. 

If you’re searching for ways to style leather leggings, look no further as we’ve compiled the best 7 outfits.

With a White Button Down Shirt

A crisp and clean white oxford shirt creates a tuxedo effect when worn with a sharp pair of leather leggings, making this two-piece outfit look instantly polished and put together. It's a look that exudes confidence and ease. Add bold gold hoop earrings and a brightly colored clutch to complete your outfit.

Cozy Chic

With leather leggings, you don’t have to compromise style for comfort - you can actually have both. To balance out your ensemble from looking too casual, try wearing your favorite athleisure or oversized loungewear with fitted leather leggings for an effortless look that still packs plenty of punch. 

Leather on Leather

If looking like a rebellious rockstar girlfriend is your summer aesthetic of choice, wear tight leather leggings with an Angelina Jolie-inspired leather corset. This look may appear daring and edgy, but it's surprisingly simple to assemble. For extra visual contrast, try pairing your leggings with an edgy top like a vest or corset.

In Bold Burgundy

Instead of reaching for your trusty standard black leather leggings, why not add an unexpected pop of color with a beautiful burgundy pair like the ones above? Pair them with a matching blouse and outerwear for a knockout monochromatic look. Not only will you maintain the comfort of the leggings, but you'll easily stand out from the crowd.

In Patent Leather

If you’re like Wednesday Addams and are allergic to any hue other than black, go for a high-shine patent iteration to create an attention-commanding outfit. A cropped black sweater will highlight the leggings’ silhouette, while gold statement jewelry adds even more glitz and glam to the shiny sheen of patent leather.

With a Blazer and Cami

Reach for your favorite fitted blazer and lace to wear with your leather leggings for the perfect office siren look. This certainly can become the go-to transitional outfit in your wardrobe that can easily take you from the boardroom to brunch in a breeze.

With a Structured Peplum Blouse

Thanks to leather leggings’ pin-straight fit, sculptural shirts are their best friend. Instead of going for an all-around fitted look, try on a top with more structure. For instance, a tweed peplum shirt takes center stage when anchored by skinny leggings, allowing you to swing about in your look as if the sidewalk was your personal runway.

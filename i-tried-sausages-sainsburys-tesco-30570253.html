'I tried sausages from Sainsbury's, Tesco and M&amp;S to see which was best'

With it (supposedly) being summer, there is only one thing on people's minds.

Yep you've guessed it... BBQs.

When we eventually get a bit of sunshine, there's nothing more that us Brits love than whacking a banger on the barbie and tucking in.

However, you have to get the right sausage if you really want to impress your guests.

And luckily for you, I've tried out five different brands on the market to find the ultimate banger.

And there was one clear winner.

Who was it? Well read on and you can find out below.

Fifth position: Richmond 8 Thick Pork Sausages

I've never been a fan of Richmond sausages in general and they didn't do it for me on the BBQ either I'm afraid.

I find they have a bit of a bland taste and aren't very girthy either.

Definitely the worst out of the bangers I tried to be honest and I won't be buying them again.

Fourth position: Sainsbury's British Pork Sausages, Taste the Difference x6 400g

I do love a 'taste the difference' or 'extra special' range, however I wasn't totally impressed with Sainsbury's offering.

If I'm paying a little more, I want to notice it, and I just found that they didn't taste any different from the supermarket's normal range to be frank.

The Sainsbury's sausages were tasty enough, but different give me that extra pizazz I was after, which is why they have come never the bottom of my banger list.

Third position: M&S Select Farms British 6 Farmhouse Pork Sausages

We all know Marks & Spencer do some tasty food – and its sausages are no different.

However, they only made it in third position on my list as there were still a few other brands I found tastier.

You can tell the meat in the M&S Select Farms British 6 Farmhouse Pork Sausages is of good quality and I enjoyed the hint of lemon zest in these sausages.

Would definitely get them again.

Runner-up: Tesco Finest 6 Pork & Bramley Apple Sausages 400G

I really love the Tesco Finest sausages and the Pork & Bramley Apple ones are definitely my favourite.

They're very meaty and juicy and just give you something extra which makes them stand out from the rest. T

he sausages are usually on a Clubcard deal too, which means you don't break the bank buying them.

Winner: Heck 97% British Pork Sausages 400g

Although Tesco was a close second, Heck just about sneaked out in front with their 97% British Pork Sausages.

These have a lovely taste to them and you hardly get any fat come out – so you know it's good meat.

The light casing also crisps up perfectly on a BBQ, which is why they were my number one option to throw on the grill.

Heck also do BBQ bundles for £29.99, which are perfect to save money and give you a variety for your barbie food.

So what are you waiting for? Call your family and friends and get sizzling those sausages immediately.

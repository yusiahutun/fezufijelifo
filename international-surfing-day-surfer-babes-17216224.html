Meet the surfer babes of Instagram: Nine ladies who are making waves online

Today marks International Surfing Day.

The event that celebrates surfers and the oceans they make waves in.

And it’s likely to be a grand occasion, as the sport has seen a huge spike in popularity in recent years.

We’ve seen surfers attract huge social media followings – and some have even landed themselves spots on Love Island.

So which sportswomen should you be following on Instagram?

Instagram handle: @lucierosedonlan

Followers: 790,000

About: Lucie, 21, is a surfer from Newquay.

She first took up the sport when she was 13-years-old, which sparked her love for the sea.

After years of honing her skills, she became the youngest female ISA Surf Judge in the UK.

The blonde bombshell has also been working as a model for two years.

You’re likely to recognise her, as she’s currently spending time in the Love Island villa.

Instagram handle: @lauraloucrane

Followers: 580,000

About: Laura Crane is a 24-year-old from Devon.

She’s surfed in the Great Britain team and qualified for the world series twice.

The influencer has used her platform to speak about mental health and eating disorders in the past.

She’s also another beauty to catch the eye of Love Island producers, as she was picked to be on the series in 2018.

Instagram handle: @elliejeancoffey

Followers: 1 million

About: The 24-year-old lives on the Gold Coast, Australia.

In 2017, she was ranked 97th in the QS world rankings.

When she’s not riding the waves, she works as a model.

The blonde babe has endorsed a number of different brands, from Billabong to TJ Swim.

Instaram handle: @hollydazecoffeyyy

Followers: 933,000

About: Sister to Ellie, the 21-year-old also has a love for the sea.

In 2011, she reigned victorious at the ASR Australian Junior Surfing Title.

The sporty lass also enjoys playing football and skateboarding.

In the past, she’s promoted brands including Braking Wax and Brandon Boards on Instagram.

Instagram handle: @alguilmant

Followers: 231,000

About: While Ale doesn’t have many surfing snaps on Instagram, she does have plenty of bikini pictures.

The multi-talented model has also turned her hand to acting.

She has starred in TV shows including Here on Earth and Habia Una Vez.

Instagram handle: @alanarblanchard

Followers: 1.8 million

About: The mum-of-one has won six different surfing championships, including the T&C Women’s Pipeline Championships.

She recently got engaged to her partner Jack Freestone, the father to her son Banks Harvey.

Alana regularly posts vlogs and videos to YouTube.

These clips focus on weight loss, family life and days at the beach.

Instagram handle: @lindsayperry

Followers: 40,000

About: Music-loving Lindsay dedicates her life to creativity.

When she’s not surfing, she enjoys writing and modelling.

As well as this, she’s working on recording her own album filled with original songs.

Instagram handle: @lauraenever

Followers: @424,000

About: The Aussie surfer has scooped numerous accolades for her skills.

She was crowned ASP Women’s World Champion in 2009, before making her professional debut two years later.

Laura takes competing very seriously, surfing daily and cross-training 4-5 times a week.

She’s also currently working on a film that “ventures into the world of big wave surfing”.

Instagram handle: @briannacope

Followers: 110,000

About: The beach babe is from Kauai, Hawaii.

Previously, she was ranked 69th in Women’s QS.

When she’s not surfing, she enjoys selling original jewellery designs from her Etsy shop.

Let's have an insight into her life.

An American actress, Jen Lilley, is known for her works in movies and TV shows. She is also a singer who got the National Education award in Guatemala since she gives multiple concerts for underprivileged Guatemalan youth.

Jen Lilley Married Life With Jason Wayne

37-years-old actress Jen Lilley has been married to Jason Wayne since 2007. They met each other and started dating in 2005. They make their relationship private for two years. Later, on 26 May 2007, they tight the knot. The sweet couple’s marriage will melt your heart as her own dating app matched her with a man who had had several catastrophic encounters with her. In addition to loving each other, Jason and Jen are also committed to helping thousands of troubled children in the foster care system. 

Not only committed in Childhelp, but they also started fostering a little boy Kayden in 2016, who was four months old while adopting. Jen was very happy, and she even posted about it,

“I have so much peace in my heart knowing that this time, when I come back, he won’t be my foster son, he’ll be my soon to be adopted son."

And in June 2019, they adopted Kayden officially. After adopting him, they again started another child, Jeffrey, during which Jen was pregnant. After one month in July, Jen gave birth to their biological daughter at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles after 22 hours of labor. They named their daughter Julie. Jen said to PEOPLE about her daughter “a super-happy and relaxed baby” and also shared that “aside from some swelling, I feel like myself.” 

The couple went through the heartbreak of a 12-week miscarriage in 2018 due to which made Jen very sad. She even posted about it, saying, 

“When it happened, I was devastated and felt so alone. I was 12 weeks along, just a week from telling my family, and beyond excited.”

When their daughter was born, it was like a special blessing for the couple as it was really hard for Jen to endure 22 hours of labor, but instead of that, she said, 

 “I had a lot of peace about it, but at the same time, all I could think was, ‘The baby’s been in distress pretty much from the getgo, and now she has no air.’”

On 30 October 2020, the couple officially adopted Jeffrey, which Jen shared on Instagram captioning, 

“Jeffrey is the kindest, sweetest, most nurturing, and adorable little boy anyone could ever hope for. He’s the perfect brother, the perfect yes man, and he has the best heart. Jeffrey means God’s divine peace, and while I did not name him, his name is so perfectly fitting. He fills our home with peace, love, and joy, and my heart has never been so full.”

An actress, Jen Lilley, was born on 4 August 1984 in Roanoke, Virginia, USA. Her birth name is Jennifer Elizabeth Lilley, and people also call her Jen or Jenny.

She stands for 5 feet 3 inches (1.6m) and weighs 53 kg. Her father’s name is Vince Lilley, a judge, and Her mother’s name is Ellen Lilley, a director of marketing/event planner. She is one of four children of her parents. She has one younger sister, a younger brother, and one elder brother.

She completed high school education at Cave Spring High School in Virginia and later joined the University of Virginia for further study. While studying at the University of Virginia, she started her acting journey from her first film named The Loss of Life.

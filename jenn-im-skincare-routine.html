Jenn Im Walks Us Through Her Entire Beauty Routine

Jenn Im is a vlogger, Instagram sensation, and fashion designer—but she's also a bonafide skincare pro. In this episode of Go To Bed With Me, Im walks us through her "Korean-inspired" nighttime skin routine.

Shop Jenn's Routine

"Normally it's ten steps, but since I'm traveling I minimized it to seven," she says. "Minimalism is great."

First up is a makeup removing oil-based cleanser from Clinique. "It is well-loved, I have scooped a lot out of it," she notes. "It melts everything off. At first it feels weird to rub this all up in your business. I swear it won't break you out, it just removed everything and melts everything away."

After rinsing the balm off, she moves on to another cleanser. "K-beauty is all about double cleansing. It's just a fancy word for saying you wash your face twice," she says, reaching for a jelly ball cleanser from Bliss. "It's really fun to sue, you put it over hot water and lather this baby up and put it on your face."

After patting her skin dry, Im likes to layer on two products from SK-II, the lotion and essence. "This is basically a toner. I am just going to pat it on my face, it balances all the ph levels on your skin," she says as applying the lotion. "And don't forget your neck."

The SK-II Essence Im called her "holy grail" of beauty. "Essences put the amino acids and other things good for your skin back into your face," Im notes. "You want to make a pool of it in your hand and immediately slather it all over your skin and it's going to drink it all up."

Then comes a redness-reducing and calming serum from Dr. Jart. "I like to just put one of these drops and apply it on my face. It smells so good, it smells like Tiger Balm. It's very cooling and it reminds me of my mom. I feel like my mom is here," she says.

The last two steps are all about hydration. She uses a travel size La Mer cream to "lock all the products we put on our face and seal the deal" and the Sisley Eye Contour cream which she says is "very thick" and helps prevent wrinkles.

Jeong’s professional path

Jeong made his Bundesliga debut with Bayern Munich. Bayern Munich signed him on June 30, 2017. On November 27, 2018, he made his Bayern Munich debut in the UEFA Champions League. When he was a member of Bayern, the squad won the Premier League International Cup. It was his first international championship. Joeng signed a four-year deal with SC Freiburg on June 19, 2019. On August 10, 2019, he made his debut in Freiburg. In a friendly match against Japan, he made his debut for the South Korean national team.

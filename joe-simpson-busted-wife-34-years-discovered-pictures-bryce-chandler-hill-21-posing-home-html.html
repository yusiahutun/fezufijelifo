The 34 year union between Jessica Simpson's parents was destroyed after her mother found pictures of 21-year-old Bryce Chandler Hill and her husband posing in their home.

Suspicious about seeing a boy she had never met pictured in various places throughout the Encino mansion, Tina, 52, immediately flew New York City to confront her husband.

Made over: Joe Simpson, continuing his flamboyant transformation, was spotted in Los Angeles on Monday morning

It was August when 54-year-old Joe had some explaining to do in the lobby of a Manhattan hotel, according to Radar Online.

'Joe got busted after Tina found some modeling pictures of Bryce in her home,' a source close to the Simpson family exclusively told RadarOnline.com.

'They were taken in and around the house and she was suspicious because she didn’t have a clue who the boy was.

'Joe and Tina’s marriage was in trouble and they had a make or break vacation booked in Hawaii, so she didn’t ask Joe any questions at that point.

Changes: Jessica and Ashlee's father is experimenting with his hair, clothes and general style

'However, when they returned to Los Angeles Joe bizarrely got on a plane to New York, just six hours after they had landed in California.

Tina enlisted daughter Jessica’s best friend CaCee Cobb for help in finding out who the boy was.

'CaCee uncovered some personal pictures of Joe and Bryce posing together and showed Tina.

The game was up: Pictures of Bryce Chandler Hill with Joe Simpson prompted his wife Tina to ask all the right questions, it's being alleged

'Tina wanted answers,' the source revealed. 'She flew to New York to make an unannounced visit to Joe.

'When she got there, she called him down to the lobby and they thrashed it out.'

In a further revelation it has been revealed that Joe had 'lavished expensive gifts on his toyboy' Bryce.

A source told Radar: 'Over a 12-month period, Tina saw that Joe was splashing thousands of dollars in designer stores... staying in luxury hotels and buying tickets for concerts. What was worse, the money that Joe had freely spent on Bryce was from a mutual account he shared with Tina.'

It has been claimed that Ashlee and her sister Jessica are struggling to cope in the wake of the divorce announcement.

A source said: 'Jessica had absolutely no clue that her father was gay, not even the slightest inkling.
'It was a complete shock to the system, she feels terribly for her mom who was left reeling by the announcement and her sister Ashlee has also taken the news quite badly.'

Joe's appearance in particular caused a bit of a stir - as he appeared to have had something of a flamboyant makeover.

Last week, soon after claims surrounding Joe's sexuality emerged, the news broke that Tina had filed for divorce in September.

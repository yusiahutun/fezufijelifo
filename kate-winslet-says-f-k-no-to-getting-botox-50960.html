Kate Winslet Says F**k No to Getting Botox

Unlike some other Hollywood actresses (we won’t name names), Kate Winslet isn’t willing to get Botox to maintain her youthful looks.

When the 39-year-old actress was asked in a ‘Harper’s Bazaar UK’ interview if she has ever tried the popular cosmetic procedure, the ‘Titanic’ star replied with a definitive, “Oh f–k no!”

In 2011, Kate similarly expressed her disinterest in going under the knife by stating she would “never give in” to the pressures to counteract aging.

Kate at the ‘Divergent’ premiere in March 2014.

“It goes against my morals, the way that my parents brought me up and what I consider to be natural beauty,” she said.

“I am an actress, I don’t want to freeze the expression of my face,” Kate added.

Kate with Emma (left) in 1996 and with Rachel (right) in 2001.

In August of that year, Kate joined forces with fellow actresses Emma Thompson, 55, and Rachel Weisz, 44, to form what they refer to as “The British Anti-Cosmetic Surgery League,” to band together against women in Hollywood undergoing unnecessary surgical procedures.

“I’m not fiddling about with myself,” Emma also told ‘The Telegraph.’ “We’re in this awful youth-driven thing now where everybody needs to look 30 at 60.”

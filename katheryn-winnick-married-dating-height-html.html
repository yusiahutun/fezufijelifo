Katheryn Winnick Husband, Height, Net Worth

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: August 08, 2019

Best recognized for her role in Vikings as the wife of Ragnar Lothbrok, Katheryn Winnick is a Canadian actress. For her fantastic performance on the show, she has been nominated for prestigious awards like Critics' Choice Television Awards, Golden Maple Awards, WIM Awards, and Canadian Screen Awards.

However, that's not all. Besides being an actress, Katheryn is a licensed self-defense instructor and a licensed bodyguard. She is known to teach martial arts to fellow actors on film sets. 

Bio: Age, Height, Family

Born as Katerena Anna Vinitska on 17 December 1977, the actress hails from a small town called Etobicoke in Ontario, Canada.  Katheryn is the only daughter of her parents: Lesya Winnicki and Roman Winnicki. For siblings, she has two brothers, namely Adam and Markjan Winnick. Like her, Markjan is also an actor.

Katheryn completed her education from York University. Later, to polish her skills in acting, Katheryn joined the William Esper Studio in New York City.

As for the actress' physical appearance, she is a beautiful blonde. Katheryn - age 41 - stands with an average height of 5 feet 6 inches (1.67 m). She dawns a beautiful slim curvy body figure that weighs in at 55kgs.

Dating Or Married?

Even with the spotlight of the entertainment industry on her, Katheryn has managed to keep the details of her personal life as discreet as possible. However, that hasn't stopped fans from making assumptions. 

Back in 2015, the actress was rumored to be romantically linked with filmmaker and businessman, Nick Loeb. The rumors gained more steam when they were sighted together out on a date at the Sunset Tower Hotel in Los Angeles. But, the gossips ended after Katheryn dismissed the claims during an interview with US Weekly.

Katheryn Winnick and Travis Fimmel at Comic-Con International in 2015 (Photo: zimbio.com)

The same year, Katheryn was linked with her fellow co-star, Travis Fimmel. Appearing as husband and wife on the show, fans could do nothing but gush about the couple.

However, neither, Travis not Katheryn has commented anything on the matter of their relationship. But, with the series coming to an end, fans will have a reason to suspect them as a couple if they remain together.

Katheryn Winnick's Career 

Katheryn made her first appearance with the recurring role of Holly Benson in 1999 with Student Bodies. Soon after, the actress also lent her talent to movies like Biohazardous and Two Weeks Notice.

Since then, the Canadian actress has appeared in many TV shows and movies. She also starred in Radio Free Albemuth, Choose, Killers, and Love & Other Drugs in 2010. The actress got her big break in 2013 when she joined the cast of Vikings.

On the show for the History Channel, Katheryn brought life to the role of Lagertha. The part has undoubtedly brought out her acting skills for the world to see and helped her climb the ladder of showbiz.

With her newfound fame from Vikings, the actress has managed to land roles left and right in Hollywood. She has also appeared in Person of Interest (2015), The Dark Tower (2017), and Speed Kills (2018).

Apart from movies and TV shows, Katheryn has also lent her voice in many projects. She voiced the character of Marie Fischer in the popular video game, Call of Duty: WWII. With two decades of a rewarding career, Katheryn has undoubtedly cemented her name alongside the elites in Hollywood. Throughout her career, Katheryn has amassed a net worth of $1 million with her acting prowess.

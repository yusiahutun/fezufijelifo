Kathleen McCrone: The Lawyer Wife of Wayne Newton

Kathleen McCrone, a distinguished American lawyer, gained widespread recognition as the beloved wife of Wayne Newton. Her husband, Newton, is an iconic American singer and actor. He was one of the most popular singers in the United States from the mid-to-late 20th century.

Famous as “Mr. Las Vegas,” McCrone’s husband is well-known for songs like “1972’s Daddy,” “Don’t You Walk So Fast,” “Red Roses for a Blue Lady,” and “Years.” Although McCrone is famous as the iconic singer’s wife, she has a successful career.

Here’s a look into Kathleen McCrone’s life, employment, and marriage to Newton.

How Old Is Wayne Newton’s Wife?

Kathleen McCrone was born on March 21, 1964, in North Olmsted, Ohio, to parents William J. McCrone and Marilyn Furman. Hence, she is 59 years old. Her father, a respected figure in the legal world, served as a federal judge at the Cuyahoga Court of Common Pleas and Rocky River Municipal Court in Ohio. In addition, the singer’s wife grew up with two sisters, Mary McCrone and Tricia Clift McCrone.

McCrone lost her sister, Mary, 2009 to a heart attack, adding an element of sorrow to her life. Her father died of cancer some years later. Inspired by her father’s esteemed career as a judge, Kathleen McCrone pursued higher education.

However, details about her educational background and achievements remain undisclosed. Her dedication and hard work made her a successful civil and criminal litigation lawyer.

How Did Kathleen McCrone Meet Her Husband?

In 1990, McCrone’s path crossed with Newton backstage at one of his Las Vegas shows. A genuine friendship blossomed, and the pair soon started dating. However, after three years of dating, they took the next step, exchanging vows in a lavish and public wedding ceremony on April 9, 1994. 

The couple had a fairy-tale wedding and rode into their ceremony in a horse-drawn carriage. Their extravagant wedding ceremony occurred at Newton’s 53-acre ranch in Nevada, United States, with over 200 guests witnessing their union.

Their marriage has withstood the test of time. McCrone and Newton have kept their relationship scandal-free for almost three decades, enjoying each other’s company and making numerous public appearances. 

In 2014, McCrone and her husband attended the Academy of Country Music Awards and performed together at the 2015 Billboard Music Awards in Las Vegas. Their love remains strong, evident in their social media displays of affection and support for one another.

How Many Children Does Kathleen McCrone and Her Husband Have?

The couple have a daughter. In 2002, McCrone and her husband welcomed their daughter, Lauren Ashley Newton, into their lives through a surrogate mother. However, Lauren is now 21, and her parents’ loving guidance has shaped her into a promising young adult. Their bond as a family is a testament to the love and devotion they share.

Is Wayne Newton Married?

Yes, he is. The “Red Roses for a Blue Lady” singer has been with his wife, Kathleen McCrone, for almost three decades. However, before marrying McCrone, Newton was previously married to Elaine Okamura in 1968, and the couple had a daughter named Erin Newton in 1976.

Erin is now 47 years old. Although Newton’s first marriage ended in divorce in 1985, it led him to find love and happiness with McCrone.

What Does Kathleen McCrone Do for a Living?

Although she is famous as Wayne Newton’s wife, she is a woman of diverse talents. McCrone’s legal acumen and dedication have led her to a successful career as a civil and criminal litigation lawyer. The inspiration drawn from her father’s legacy as a judge fueled her passion for law, and she has made a name for herself in the legal field.

In addition to her legal achievements, McCrone has also made television appearances. Notably, she was seen in “The Bachelorette” in 2018, “Celebrity Family Feud” in 2008, and “Biography” in 1999.

Kathleen McCrone, an accomplished American lawyer, has become a recognizable figure in her own right. Hence, her journey from a successful legal career to being a cherished wife and devoted mother reflects her multifaceted nature and strength of character.

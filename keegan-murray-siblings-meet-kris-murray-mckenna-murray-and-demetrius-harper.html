Keegan Murray Siblings: Meet Kris Murray, Mckenna Murray, And Demetrius Harper

Keegan Mitchell Murray is an American professional basketball player for the Sacramento Kings of the National Basketball Association. He was selected fourth overall in the 2022 NBA draft.

Meet Kris Murray, Mckenna Murray, And Demetrius Harper

Keegan Murray has a twin brother, Kris. He was named after dad Kenyon’s college teammate Chris Street, who was tragically killed in a car accident.

The twins attended Prairie High School in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, in 2019 and eventually went to DME Academy, a post-graduate basketball school in Daytona Beach, Florida. They then got scholarship offers from Iowa and played for the Hawkeyes. Kris was a sophomore on the 2021-22 roster.

Speaking about raising the boys, Michelle told The Gazette: “I’m so grateful God chose us to raise them. We spent a lot of time with the kids, reading to them. We had flashcards we did with them all the time. I taught them sign language when they were little.

“We would have a family movie night, family game night. We just loved being together and we appreciated the times we could be together. We’ve always had a calmness in our home.”

Keegan Murray also has a younger sister, McKenna, who plays basketball at Prairie.

The Murrays also adopted Demetrius Harper seven years ago. Demetrius was a star athlete at Prairie at the time and went on to play college football at Coe.

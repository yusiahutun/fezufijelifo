Kendall Jenner Launches MOON Rose Mint Toothpaste and Teeth Whitening Pen

In addition to supermodel, reality television star, and all-around It girl, Kendall Jenner is adding another title to her list: dental influencer. The star has created her own twist to MOON's core teeth-whitening products with the launch of a new Rose Mint Collection, featuring the Rose Mint Teeth Whitening Pen and Whitening Toothpaste.

Although she's been a MOON partner since 2019, this marks Jenner's first signature collection for the brand. "I'm so excited to share our new Rose Mint Toothpaste and Whitening Pen. It fits seamlessly into my oral beauty routine and the scent is very calming and soothing," Jenner tells BAZAAR.com of the new release. "The Rose Mint Collection was created as a two-step routine, but they can also be used separately. Oral care can be a chore, but hopefully this collection will inspire others to get excited about taking care of their teeth."

MOON

MOON

Both the toothpaste and whitening pen are available for less than $20. The whitening pen contains light-reflecting pigments and is infused with lavender oil as a stress reliever, strawberry extract to whiten, and honeysuckle leaf extract, which acts as an astringent. The toothpaste contains a blend of antioxidants and essential oils to remove stains, brighten your smile, and leave your mouth feeling refreshed. And unlike the majority of MOON's more minimal, streamlined products, the Rose Mint Collection features distinctive rose gold packaging that also happens to be earth-conscious.

"The Rose Mint Collection really represents my personal beauty aesthetic," Jenner continues. "Everything from the collection packaging to the rose mint flavor originally stemmed from my love for florals. The Rose Mint Toothpaste and Whitening Pen perfectly blend the wellness-inducing properties of rose with a refreshing mint flavor for a clean feeling and playful take on oral beauty. This two-step routine is simple to use and will leave you feeling pampered." Dental care with a side of pampering? Sounds dreamy to us.

The MOON x Kendall Jenner Rose Mint Collection is available to shop now online at moonoralcare.com and ulta.com.

Lady Amelia Spencer looks like a goddess in white bikini on tropical honeymoon

The niece of Princess Diana looked incredible in Melissa Odabash swimwear

    Lady Amelia Spencer is currently basking in post-wedding bliss as she suns it up in the Maldives with her husband Greg Mallet. 

    Taking to Instagram to share postcards from their slice of heaven at The Conrad Hotel on Rangali Island, Princess Diana's newlywed niece shared an influx of sun-soaked snaps from the second installment of her honeymoon - and the socialite looked nothing short of enchanting in her enviable summer wardrobe.

    "Week two of honeymoon - in paradise," penned Amelia who began her gallery of photos with a stunning bikini snap reclining on a crystal-clear infinity pool.

    Lady Amelia opted for a royal-favourite swimwear designer, Melissa Odabash, rocking the brand's 'Bahamas' bikini in a fitting bridal-white hue. Her modelesque figure looked incredible as she soaked up the sunshine, letting her mermaid-like hair trail in the water. 

    Other photos showed the loved-up duo paddleboarding in the ocean, as well as enjoying beachfront dining and romantic candlelit dinners to toast their start of forever together.

    The twin of Lady Eliza Spencer is maintaining her bridal-themed wardrobe, sharing a host of cream-coloured and pastel swim sets for friends and followers to covet as she laps up the tropical heat. In one snap, the model donned an elegant floral mini dress, complete with ruffled tiers and angelic floaty sleeves. 

    Amelia's golden blonde hair fell past her shoulders in natural waves as she stole a kiss with her fitness and nutrition coach husband under the stars. 

    "Amelia, you look so radiant and content. Enjoy you two it looks amazing there," commented a fan, as another penned: "Breathtakingly beautiful. You complete the view."

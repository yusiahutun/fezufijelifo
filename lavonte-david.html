Lavonte David (born January 23, 1990) is an American football linebacker for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for the University of Nebraska, and was drafted by the Buccaneers in the second round of the 2012 NFL Draft. He won his first Super Bowl championship on February 7, 2021, in a 31-9 victory over the Kansas City Chiefs.

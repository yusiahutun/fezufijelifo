Personally Know Macy Jenkins From Fox 4 - Is She Single Or Married?

We love Macy Jenkins. She is independent, strong, inspiring, and always uplifting those around her. But, what else do we know about Jenkins when she is not working? Is the young beautiful woman dating anyone?

Peek Into Macy Jenkins' Dating Life

The career-driven FOX4 news reporter, Macy Jenkins has already inspired a lot of young women into Journalism. Because Jenkins is that fierce woman who came a long way to catch her dreams as she has established herself as one of the committed news anchors.

However, little is known about the graduate of Syracuse University when the matter concerns her personal life. As a matter of fact, Jenkins isn't fond of talking about her love life on social media.

Fans might get glimpses of her family, colleagues, and travel diaries, but never about Macy's dating life. And, in the meantime, it looks like Jenkins is a single woman who has rarely been romantically linked to a man.

The dark-haired beauty either doesn't want the world to get its nose into her business or Jenkins is still yet to find her Prince Charming. Hopefully, her fans will find out the lovely news when Macy meets her significant half in the foreseeable days.

But, in the meantime, Macy is busy chasing her dream and enjoying her single life to the fullest.

Macy Jenkins' Family - Actress Maya Jenkins Is Her Sister

Meanwhile, the independent news reporter Macy Jenkins isn't the only popular face in her family. Well, the actress Maya Jenkins is Macy's sweet little sister who, likewise, has already achieved so much. Looks like talents smoothly run in the Jenkins family.

The talented Jenkins siblings, Macy Jenkins with her sister Maya Jenkins. SOURCE: Macy Jenkins Instagram

Jenkins' sister is an actress, best known for her roles in Muted, Famous in Love, Maya J: Like You, Black Hollywood Live: The Beat, and AfterbuzzTV's Aftershow: Famous in Love Edition. Jenkins who has also acted in dozen of short movies is also a producer, writer, and singer.

And, Macy is definitely proud of her sister's accomplishments as an artist. Both Macy and Maya are based in California and Flo Rida where they have spent the majority of their life.

In addition to Macy's background, she attended George Washington University for her BA degree before she joined Syracuse University where she attained her Master of Science degree.

And, as per her LinkedIn profile, Jenkins worked for CBS 13 Sacramento, KTHV in Arkansas, KOLR 10 Ozark's First, NCC News, KABC-TV, Oliff & Berridge, PLC, The Walt Disney Company, Penn, Schoen & Berland Associates, Orr Associates, Inc., The Climate Project and GW Office of Undergraduate Admissions, before she joined FOX44 News.

Mahesh Babu reveals he did not use tobacco for Guntur Kaaram; says he neither smokes nor encourages

Superstar Mahesh Babu was most recently seen in the movie Guntur Kaaram directed by Trivikram Srinivas. Despite the film receiving mixed-to-negative reviews from critics, it is currently running in theaters.

In a recent interview, the actor shared an interesting tidbit. He disclosed that during the filming of Guntur Kaaram, he didn't use real beedis or cigarettes. Instead, he was given an Ayurvedic beedi made from clove leaves.

Mahesh Babu reveals the use of Ayurvedic beedi in Guntur Kaaram

During the interview, Mahesh Babu was questioned about the beedis he used in the movie. The actor made it clear that he doesn't smoke and doesn't support smoking either. He shared that he was given a real beedi initially, but it gave him an instant migraine. So, he discussed it with Trivikram.

They pondered for a while and eventually settled on trying out Ayurvedic beedis. Surprisingly, he instantly fell in love with the beedi that had a refreshing minty taste when he smoked it. The best part was that these beedis were completely tobacco-free.

More about Guntur Kaaram

The makers of Guntur Kaaram had initially planned to lodge a cyber complaint against the supposed utilization of counterfeit bots on BookMyShow, which harmed their film's reputation. They view this as a deliberate attack by certain anti-film factions.

Moreover, the team of Guntur Kaaram including the two actresses Sreeleela and Meenakshii Chaudhary were recently spotted at Mahesh Babu’s residence celebrating the success of the film.

Guntur Kaaram written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas is the director’s third collaboration with Mahesh Babu since their films like Athadu and Khaleja. The film features the story of a brash and well-known figure in Guntur called Veera Venkata Ramana (Mahesh Babu) who has a strained relationship with his mother, who abandoned him when he was young.

The protagonist's inability to comprehend his mother's departure leads to his transformation into a bold and impulsive individual, becoming the central theme of the story.

As the film progresses, it unveils the reasons behind her abandonment and showcases the journey of the son and mother as they overcome various obstacles to mend their relationship.

The movie boasts a talented ensemble cast, with notable actors such as Ramya Krishnan, Jayaram, Prakash Raj, Rao Ramesh, Vennela Kishore, and many others playing pivotal roles.

Meet Feud: Capote vs the Swans star Ella Beatty, Warren Beatty and Annette Benings daughter: t

Ella Beatty, the daughter of veteran Hollywood actors Warren Beatty and Annette Bening, is following in her parents’ acting footsteps having just made her TV debut in Feud: Capote Vs. the Swans, which released its first two episodes on January 31.

The 23-year-old stars in acclaimed writer-director Ryan Murphy’s latest series as Kerry O’Shea, who was the daughter of American novelist and screenwriter Truman Capote’s lover, John O’Shea, per WWD.

On January 23, the young actress chatted to WWD on the red carpet during the premiere, praising the show’s costumes. “Lou [Eyrich], who does the costumes, is a genius, and as an actress it’s really helpful when you put something on and you imagine and you completely feel immersed in the person that you’re playing.”

The series also features Naomi Watts as Babe Paley, Diane Lane as Nancy “Slim” Keith, Tom Hollander as Capote, and Demi Moore as Ann Woodward.

Here’s what you need to know about Ella Beatty as she emerges into the Hollywood spotlight:

Ella Beatty is the daughter of Warren Beatty and Annette Bening

Ella Beatty hails from a famous family. Her dad Warren Beatty, 86, is an American actor and filmmaker who has starred in a variety of films since the 1960s, including Dick Tracy, Bugsy and Rules Don’t Apply. Ella’s mother Annette Bening, 65, who has been married to Warren since 1992, has been in American Beauty, 20th Century Women, The Kids are all Right and The Grifters, to name a few.

She has three older siblings

Ella is the youngest of four siblings, per People.

Her eldest sibling, Stephen, is 32; Benjamin is 29, and her sister Isabel is 27. All four of them have remained under the radar.

She studied at Juilliard

As reported by WWD, Beatty studied at Juilliard, a prestigious performing arts university in New York. She reportedly graduated in 2022, after training in the school’s drama division, according to the source.

She hangs out with other nepo babies

Beatty hasn’t made many public appearances yet, but she’s starting to gain recognition thanks to her new role in Feud: Capote Vs. the Swans.

That doesn’t mean she hasn’t crossed paths with other celebrities during her rise to stardom though. Per British media, she has been pictured hanging out with fellow nepo baby Vito Schnabel, the 37-year-old ex-boyfriend of model Heidi Klum and son of artist Julian Schnabel in February of last year.

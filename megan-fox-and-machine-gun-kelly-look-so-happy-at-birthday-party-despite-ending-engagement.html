Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly 'look so happy' at birthday party despite ending engagement

Despite the two calling off their engagement, Megan Fox was seen celebrating Machine Gun Kelly’s 34th birthday at his A-List-filled bash.

The celebrity couple have had a rocky relationship over the past couple of years, and just last month, Megan Fox admitted they had ended their engagement. Although the Hollywood star wasn’t willing to explain the details, she did say she believes Machine Gun Kelly is “still her twin soul,” and it looks like the pair are still on great terms.

Megan Fox and MGK pose together at his birthday – despite ending their engagement

Machine Gun Kelly took to his Instagram to let fans inside his celebrity-filled A-List bash, and amongst the slideshow, was an adorable pic of himself and Megan. The two looked in good spirits as they smiled next to each other in front of a candle-lit backdrop.

The Jennifer’s Body star crouched down next to the singer, as she stunned in all black, letting her long purple locks hang loose.

MGK, real name Colson Baker, wore a baby blue T-Shirt with a picture of rapper Nelly’s face on the front.

In another video, circulating X, formerly known as Twitter, Fox could be seen standing by MGK as he took the mic.

“They look happy asf,” commented one fan.

Another wrote: “They ain’t together. It was his bday and she just wants to make sure he is okay. Bet she left that night.”

“I’m glad they’re still friends at least,” a third penned.

The actress gives her advice for ‘single girls’ this summer

Although she wasn’t sure if she was the best person for the job, the Transformers star outlined her advice for the single girls this Summer, while at Coachella.

“My advice is just to learn a skill or develop a hobby, and do not waste your energy on boys,” she told E! News. “All they’re gonna do is drain you, just move on. Invest in yourself,” the 37-year-old concluded.

Last month, on the Call Her Daddy podcast, the mom of three admitted she never got to experience her “young single era,” as she entered her relationship with ex-husband, Brian Austin Green at just 18 years old.

Soon after, she got into a relationship with MGK, so maybe now it’s time for her to finally enter her single era!

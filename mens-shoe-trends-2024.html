Investing in fresh fashion is a great way to usher in the new year. Although selecting the best denim or outerwear might feel a little more intuitive, shopping from the bottom up, aka your feet – might feel better with some guidance. Enter: the top men’s shoe trends for 2024. Between the classics and statement or dressier styles, what silhouettes will rule 2024? 

We predict loafers, running shoes, and other familiar styles to only gain traction in the new year. While the shoes spotted on high-fashion runways, top celebrities, influencers, and fashion insiders will also help shape the trends for 2024. Think Mary Janes, mules, and fashion-forward hiking shoes.

The current men’s shoe options will have you covered for casual and presentation days at work, date nights or nights with the boys, weekends lounging at home or outside, and so much more. You’ll find options in a wide range of pricing that will help elevate an outfit and ground you when you need it. Ahead, discover the nine trends to step into, guaranteed to make for one stylish 2024. And if you’d like to capitalize on one of the styles, we have recommendations for each trend to shop immediately. Good luck choosing just one.

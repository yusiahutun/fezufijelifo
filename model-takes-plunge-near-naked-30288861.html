The model claims she experienced aggression after deciding to jump with a parachute almost naked at a skydiving site in São Paulo, Brazil.

But, even though she felt super sexy, she said she was harassed and threatened by other women who were in line to jump with her.

According to her, they made cruel comments because she was wearing a bathing suit and high heels for her landing.

Even so she said she managed to change the jump instructions with the skydiving team ahead of the dive.

According to the model, the ladies booed her and told her it was not a place for "women of that type".

Cris said: "One of them was on top of me, pointing a finger in my face.

"I went in a bikini to record content for Instagram before the jump and I was going to change clothes.

The photos of the parachute jump have already attracted thousands of likes and comments.

Cris recently celebrated making six-digits on OnlyFans and explained she thought the leap was a liberating way to celebrate the achievement.

"It is unfortunate that a person's beauty is still a reason for discrimination and prejudice in the 21st century," she added.

"We hope that this type of situation will not be repeated, and that all people can enjoy their activities without being judged by their appearance."

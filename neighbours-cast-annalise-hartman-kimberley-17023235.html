But one of the most well-known Neighbours bombshells, other than Janae Timmins, is the infamous Annalise Hartman, played by Kimberley Davies.

She was on the show from 1993-96, and made a guest appearance in 2005.

Despite her leaving Ramsay Street 12 years ago, Annalise certainly left an impression.

Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban have been married since 2006. Here are some of their cutest photos together in honor of Keith Urban’s birthday.

Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban married in 2006 and have been going strong ever since. Nicole is a famed actress who won an Oscar for her in ‘The Hours’ in the early 2000s. Keith has also done incredible things in music as a Grammy winner for his country music. Nicole and Keith are both from Australia and were brought together at an event. After their marriage, the couple had two daughters together, Sunday Rose and Faith Margaret.

They’ve been known to show off some PDA at various events but have always been incredibly supportive of one another. Keith has consistently been there for his wife while attending film premieres and awards ceremonies, while Nicole has also come out to support her husband’s recognition in the music industry. They are a true Hollywood power couple, and they continue to show how strong their love is in everything that they do together.

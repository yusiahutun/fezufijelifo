18 Jean Jacket Outfits You Haven't Tried Yet

A jean jacket can elevate a good outfit to great. Whether adding it as the finishing touch to complete your look or incorporating it as a statement piece to build the rest of your ensemble, you definitely want to keep this wardrobe staple in rotation. Of course, given its popularity, finding a jean jacket outfit that feels new and original can be tricky — but not impossible.

By its very nature, denim is a casual textile. Add a layer of it to a full chiffon skirt or a lacy midi dress, and you've suddenly got a look that works as well at a posh restaurant as it does in Central Park. Not only are jean jackets perfect for in-between temperatures, but they also do a lot of work when it comes to making dreamy and exciting outfits more accessible. If you're going for a more sophisticated aesthetic, darker-wash denim will give you just that. Because jean jackets come in various fits and washes, the styling possibilities are seemingly endless. To show you just what we mean, we've rounded up our favorite looks and tips for wearing this classic outerwear option.

Keep scrolling for jean jacket outfits that are bound to turn heads.

Over a Matching Set

Inject casual vibes into a matching set that feels a bit too preppy or overdone by adding an oversized jean jacket. It will balance out the look and keep it from skewing busy.

Over an LBD

Create some interest with your classic LBD by adding a denim layer. A distressed denim jacket modernizes this style, making it feel casual and cool. Pair with your footwear of choice.

With Thigh-High Boots

Another great 'fit that benefits from an oversized jean jacket is a thigh-high boots and denim combo. It doesn't really matter what you wear underneath your jean jacket with this look (although we recommend a mini skirt or shorts to underscore the contrast between footwear and outerwear).

With a Denim Maxi Skirt

Move over, denim mini skirts! The maxi is here and ready for her close-up. A maxi skirt's floor-skimming hemline adds a level of sophistication to the flirtiness of its mini counterpart.

Under a Leather Jacket

The weather can be unpredictable, which makes a great case for wearing a denim jacket as a secondary layer under a leather jacket. To make this approach work, you'll want to pair your most close-fitting jean jacket beneath roomier outerwear.

Over the Shoulders

Not only is it worth considering what jean jacket to wear, but also how to wear it. We're partial to sliding it over your shoulders or halfway down for an effortlessly cool vibe.

With an Airy Cover-Up

You don't have to banish your favorite flowing beach cover-up to the back of your closet until spring break rolls around. Repeat after us: "Every day is a beachwear day if you've got a jean jacket to throw on over your outfit."

Under Your Outerwear

A casual denim layer provides a nice contrast to elegant outerwear — not to mention it will help to keep you warm. If there's rain in the forecast, swap your winter coat for a trench coat instead.

Over a Duster

If you can't quite squeeze your jean jacket under your fave trench, there are other ways to combine these two beloved pieces of outerwear. Try pairing a duster with an oversized jean jacket for a layered look that plays with length and volume.

With Boho Accessories

Bring a free-spirited touch to a jean jacket outfit by including western and boho-inspired accessories. Warm brown leather, fringe, brassy buckles, and metalwork can bring a sense of whimsy and adventure to a jean jacket outfit.

With a Full Sheer Skirt

Give yourself some coverage while hopping on the sheer trend that's taking over the world by tossing a jean jacket over your fave sheer separates. You can also use this style of jean jacket outfit to bring some casual energy to an otherwise overtly formal look.

Over a Romantic Frock

We all have that one dress in our closet that we want to wear all the time but might be just too cocktail to wear every day. Please immediately throw on that dress, your strappiest open-toed heels, and an oversized denim jacket. Voila! You've got yourself a casual look that can take you from brunch to happy hour to a picnic in the park.

With a Flouncy Dress

For a style that's pure spring, there's almost no better combination than a ruffled dress in a playful fabric and a jean jacket. The sturdy ankle booties keep the outfit from feeling overly sweet. To adapt to fall, throw on some tights and knee-high boots.

With Denim Accessories

We all know that Canadian tuxedos can be beautifully executed, but, in our humble opinion, not enough attention is given to the denim accessories that come into play when a look is all jeans, all the time. Next time you pull on a jean jacket, think outside of the box when it comes to creating visual cohesion by including a denim baseball cap or handbag in your ensemble.

As a Bracket

When you're looking for a change from your oversized jean jacket, reach for a mini version instead. Less is more with this look, meaning a bra will pair perfectly with it. The result? An on-trend bracket creation that we'll be dreaming about for days.

With Shearling

When temperatures drop, you'll want an outfit that keeps you warm without compromising style. The solution? A shearling jean jacket. Coordinate with a turtleneck and a leather skirt for the ultimate fall outfit.

With Some Bling

Jean jackets may be casual, but an embellished version will dress it up a bit. So as not to take away from the statement piece, combine it with a basic tee and denim bottoms.

With Wide-Leg Trousers

A dark wash denim jacket wears perfectly against wide-leg trousers, especially those with lighter tones. This look is the eptime of casual sophistication.

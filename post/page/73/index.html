Vegan Japanese tomato salad with soy-onion dressing - recipe

published on August 28, 2024
For this dish, try to use as colourful a selection of ripe, sweet tomatoes as possible. The so-called heirloom varieties can be good (although very expensive), but if they're not in season, cherry tomatoes, and the oval and pear-shaped small tomatoes, are usually better than regular large supermarket varieties. If you're serving fewer people, use half the amount of tomatoes (or less), but make the full recipe for the dressing.

I Own 30+ Pairs of Earrings, but I Wear These Delicate Hoops 24/7

As someone who shops for a living, you can imagine my love for all things fashion, and I certainly can’t pass up anything that sparkles. My affinity for shiny things means my jewelry collection is quite extensive, particularly earrings. 

I have eight ear piercings and love mixing and matching different yellow-gold styles for a dreamy earscape. I rarely take my earrings out when I shower or sleep, so I’m always on the hunt for pieces with longevity that won’t tarnish quickly. My latest obsession is the Circlet Hoop Earrings from Pura Vida, which go with everything. They are the epitome of quiet luxury while still being reasonably priced for demi-fine jewelry at just $45.

Pura Vida Circlet Hoop Earrings

I’ve owned my fair share of pieces that quickly become lackluster, even those at a higher price point, and have been disappointed in the quality. Pura Vida’s demi-fine collection falls right in the affordable range for me and lives up to its anti-tarnish claims. After owning these for four months — and wearing them daily — they’re still as shiny as the day I got them. 

The earrings are handcrafted using hypoallergenic sterling silver and 18-karat gold plating that doesn’t tarnish. This isn’t my first piece from the brand’s demi-fine collection; I also own the Sunburst Heart Bracelet, and it’s held up through countless showers, pool swims, and hand-washing without any change in color. Since I’m such a fan of this timeless and high-quality collection, I certainly will be gifting pieces to others in the future. 

Sunburst Heart Bracelet

For my second and third ear piercings, this huggie style is perfect since they sit comfortably around my ear lobe without dangling too much (or clanking against my other piercings). The white topaz stones are classic and timeless and “go with everything,” according to one shopper. Another reviewer loves the hoops since they can dress them “up or down,” and they complement any outfit. 

Grab your own pair of Circlet Hoop Earrings at Pura Vida, and keep scrolling for other shopper-loved styles from the brand’s demi-fine collection.

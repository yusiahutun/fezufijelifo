Phillip Sweet's Wife Rebecca Runs ToC Instagram From Grammys

Taste of Country fans got to experience the 2016 Grammy Awards from an insider point of view when Rebecca Sweet — wife of Little Big Town’s Phillip Sweet — took over our Instagram account on Monday night (Feb. 15). Sweet shared photos showing what it looks like to get ready for such a big night, as well as a VIP's POV of the red carpet, selfies with her husband and LBT bandmates, Little Big Town's performance and award acceptance, and even a post-ceremony frame of her tired feet!

Rebecca Sweet runs her own lifestyle blog titled Sweet Ladi Jane, which offers her personal flair through food, travel, fashion and art. Check out her photos below to get the full Grammys experience, and be sure to follow her page!

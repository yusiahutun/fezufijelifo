Reese Witherspoon discusses her first nude scenes in new movie Wild

She made her name in the movie world with girl-next-door roles, from the adorably ditzy Elle Woods in Legally Blonde to the chaste Annette Hargrove in Cruel Intentions.

But nowadays, Reese Witherspoon is taking on more mature parts, and performs her first nude scenes in forthcoming movie Wild, based on Cheryl Strayed's memoir.

'I had to make a choice when I decided to do the film on how much I wanted to bare,' Reese told E! News. 'I have a real story to tell here because Cheryl was completely honest when she wrote her book, even the parts of herself which weren't as palatable or likeable, she told every part of her truth. It was scary and challenging.'

Nudity: Reese Witherspoon told E! she agreed to strip off for scenes in her new film Wild 

The film is based on Strayed's book Wild: From Lost to Found on the Pacific Crest Trail which details her decision to hike more than a thousand miles alone following her divorce; the death of her mother and recovery from drug use.

'I felt the difficultly of the emotional scenes. It's not just a movie about somebody walking. It's really about someone who feels profoundly hurt and lost,' said Reese. 

'Sexuality and drug use. All those things were really challenging.' 

Talking things out: Reese appeared with co-star Laura Dern (left) and the book's author Cheryl Strayed (right) to discuss the new film

Pensive: The star has been generating Oscar buzz for her new intrepid role 

But the role also clearly had its physical demands. 

'The idea of being out in the wilderness and carrying this 60lb backpack on my back with a pair of shorts on and a T-shirt when it was really cold [was a challenge] she confessed.

But the star, who gave the interview alongside Cheryl and Laura Dern - who plays her mother via flashbacks in the film -  was not encouraged to get in shape for the new role.

'Donuts lots of donuts,' she laughed when quizzed about her preparation. '[Director] Jean-Marc Vallée wanted me to be as green as Cheryl was when she began her hike.

'I wasn't even supposed to hike, He was like "I don't even want you to hike."'

With my girls: Laura Dern posted this snapshot showing herself, Reese and Cheryl at the press junket 

Physical demands: Reese said that carrying a 60lb backpack and wearing shorts and a T-shirt was something of a physical challenge while filming 

In character: Reese as Cheryl Strayed in Wild, which is released in cinemas on December 5 

Reese's portrayal has won favour with Cheryl, who revealed the Walk The Line actress was her first choice to portray her.

'I knew her to be a talented actress with wide range,' Cheryl told E! 'Something I always felt with Reese was that no matter what character she was playing, I felt her presence. She was at the top of my list.' 

Director Vallée has also been full of praise for Reese's performance.

'It's beautiful to see her let go and get out of her comfort zone and say, "I am going to produce it, star in it and I am going to do it in a way that's going to be tough and I'm going to be naked and raw, both physically and emotionally,"' he recently told the website.

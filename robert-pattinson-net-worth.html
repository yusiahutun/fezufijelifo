Robert Pattinson Net Worth 2022: The Batman, Twilight Salaries

From Edward Cullen to Batman, Robert Pattinson’s net worth reveals how much he made as some of Hollywood’s most iconic leading men.

Pattison, whose full name is Robert Douglas Thomas Pattinson, got his big break after starring as Edward Cullen in the Twilight franchise from 2008 to 2012. The franchise—which included five films: Twilight, New Moon, Eclipse, Breaking Dawn: Part 1, and Breaking Dawn: Part 2—came three years after Pattinson played Cedric Diggory in 2005’s Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire. His specialty for brooding antiheroes seemingly paid off. In 2019, news broke that Pattinson was cast Bruce Wayne (a.k.a. Batman) in Matt Reeves’ The Batman, which premiered in 2022.

In a 2014 interview with The Independent, Pattinson talked about why he sold his $6.37 million Los Angeles home at the time, which he bought amid his Twilight success. “The house was so amazing,” he said. “I wasn’t really thinking when I got it. I was just living in LA and had been living in and out of hotels, and you have money for the first time.” He continued, “If you are the kind of person who needs to be pushed into doing something, then Hollywood is not the right place, so I think I might be done with Los Angeles. I’ve just realised that in the past few weeks.”

Despite his roles in Twilight, The Batman and other blockbuster movie franchises, Pattinson also told the newspaper about why he’s also starred in independent films like Map of the Stars and The Lighthouse, where his salary was much, much less. “It’s quite nice doing small parts. Then the film isn’t totally reliant on what I do in it, so I get to work with who I want to work with and it’s not my fault if it doesn’t make any money.”

When it comes to how much Robert Pattinson’s net worth has increased thanks to his over decade-plus-long career in Hollywood, the results might seem even more magical than the Twilight alum‘s forays into other worlds. For everything you need to know about Robert Pattinson‘s net worth in 2022, just keep on reading.

How much did Robert Pattinson make from Twilight?

How much did Robert Pattinson make from the Twilight movies? Pattinson’s net worth Edward Cullen, a 100-year-old vampire who falls in love with a teenage girl named Bella Swan, in five Twilight movies—Twilight, New Moon, Eclipse, Breaking Dawn: Part 1, and Breaking Dawn: Part 2—from 2008 to 2012. The franchise made more than $3.3 billion worldwide by its final film in 2012. As for how much Pattinson made, Cheat Sheet reported that Pattinson made $2 million for the first Twilight movie in 2008, which was just a fraction of what he made for the final two films.

According to Vulture, Pattinson and co-leads Kristen Stewart and Taylor Lautner each earned an impressive $25 million each for final two Twilight movies: Breaking Dawn: Part 1 and Breaking Dawn: Part 2. The site also reports that they earned a percentage of the movie’s ross profits off the backend, which brought their total to around $41 million each.Vanity Fair also listed Pattison was number 15 on its “Hollywood Top 40” list in 2011, with earnings of $27.5 million in 2010 after the release of Eclipse.

How much did Robert Pattinson make from The Batman?

How much did Robert Pattinson make from The Batman? Pattinson stars as Bruce Wayne / Batman in 2022’s The Batman. Pattinson is the 12th actor to play Batman in a major movie and the second actor in the DC Extended Universe to take on the role after Ben Affleck—who played Batman in Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice, Suicide Squad, Justice League and The Flash—retired as the superhero. (Affleck made his last appearance as Batman in 2022’s The Flash.) Batman—who has also been played by actors like Adam West, Michael Keaton, Val Kilmer, George Clooney and Christian Bale—debuted in a 1939 issue of DC Comics’ Detective Comics. The comics followed Bruce, a wealthy playboy from Gotham City who fights crime as the superhero Batman after he witnesses the murder of his parents, Thomas and Martha, as a child.

In an interview with Empire in 2021, Pattinson revealed how his version of Batman is different than the actors before him. “Bruce has been hiding away,” he said. “He’s not really a socialite at all. He’s building all these little contraptions and things, just with Alfred. And even Alfred thinks he’s gone insane!”“ He continued, He’s been out every single night for two years, getting beaten up and shot and stabbed and burnt, and it shows. There’s a bullet graze on the cowl, right at the beginning. I don’t think that’s been done before.”

So what did Robert Pattinson make from The Batman? Variety reported in 2021 that Pattinson was paid $3 million for his role in The Batman, which is a lot less than other Batmans. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Christian Bale, who played Batman in Batman Begins, The Dark Knight and The Dark Knight Rises—made $9 million for Batman Begins, $10 million for The Dark Knight with a $20 million performance bonus, and $15 million for The Dark Knight Rises. Entertainment Weekly also reported in 2013 that Bale was offered $50 million for a fourth Batman movie, which he declined. The magazine also reported that he was offered $50 million to return as Batman in 2017’s Justice League, which he also declined. Deadline estimated in 2017 that Ben Affleck—who played Batman in four DC Extended Universe—made between $10 million to $20 million to play Batman as he came into the role with a base eight-figure salary.

As for other actors in the DC Extended Universe, Forbes reported in 2016 that Henry Cavill made $14 million for his role as Clark Kent / Superman in 2013’s Man of Steel, while Gal Gadot made $300,000 for her role as Diana Prince / Wonder Woman in 2017’s Wonder Woman and $10 million for 2020’s Wonder Woman: 1984, according to Variety. Deadline reported in 2017 that Ben Affleck—who played Bruce Wayne / Batman in movies like Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice and Justice League—made between $10 million and $20 million for the role. Cosmopolitan also reported in 2021 that Jason Momoa, who played Arthur Curry / Aquaman in 2018’s Aquaman, made $15 million for the role.

What is Robert Pattinson’s net worth in 2022?

What is Robert Pattinson’s net worth? Pattinson is worth $100 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Along with what he made from The Batman and Twilight movies, Robert Pattinson’s net worth also includes what he made from movies like Water For Elephants, in which he made at least more than $1 million, and Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, which allowed him to live comfortably for many years. “I spent a lot of time living off the Harry Potter money,” he told GQ in March 2022 of the movie, in which he played Cedric Diggory. “I moved to an apartment in Soho in London.”

Part of what has made Pattinson’s career so interesting is that the former teen film actor didn’t just have his sights set on blockbuster roles; he’s made it a point to star in indie films and take on passion projects of his own. Some of these include his appearance as the famous Spanish painter, Salvador Dalí, in the film Little Ashes, or Samuel Alabaster in Damsel. His knack for darker roles could also be seen in The Lighthouse, an indie film that—while nominated for several independent awards and those from local critic associations—has also amassed many careful conversations about its graphic content.

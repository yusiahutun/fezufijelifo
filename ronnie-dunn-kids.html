Country Stars With Their Kids Ronnie Dunn

This picture from 2011 shows Ronnie Dunn with his wife Janine, his daughter Haley and another unidentified female. The couple -- who've been married over 20 years -- also have a son named Jesse that just became a father to the famous singer's first grandchild in Feb. 2012.

It was the red-headed Haley that starred in Dunn's debut video as a solo artist. The singer says they used a situation she was dealing with as inspiration for 2011's 'Bleed Red.' It was her acting debut. To date, neither of Dunn's young women have shown much interest in a singing career, but it'd be hard to believe that any child of Ronnie Dunn's isn't capable of carrying a tune.

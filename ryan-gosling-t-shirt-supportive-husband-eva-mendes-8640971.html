Ryan Gosling Supports Eva Mendes in a T-Shirt With Her Book Title

Ryan Gosling and Eva Mendes may keep their relationship mostly under wraps—which means very few joint appearances—but they're constantly finding small ways to publicly show their support for each other. Case in point? While doing press for his upcoming movie The Fall Guy, Gosling wore a T-shirt featuring a subtle nod to his wife.

In a video interview with Hits Radio alongside his co-star Emily Blunt, Gosling can be seen sporting an ivory tee with the name of Mendes's forthcoming children's book, Desi, Mami, and the Never-Ending Worries, which is slated to hit shelves this fall. "By Eva Mendes" is also scrawled in smaller letters beneath the title. Gosling paired the shirt with matching jeans and a silver chain and pendant.

Blunt, on the other hand, wore an olive green corset with spiral cone cups layered over a white tee and styled with matching silk pants and chunky gold necklaces.

This isn't the first time Gosling has worn something that pays tribute to his relationship. Although Mendes did not attend the Barbie premiere alongside Gosling, the actor wore an 'E' necklace to keep her close to his heart throughout the night, per his stylist Mark Avery.

Earlier this year, Gosling gave a more obvious shoutout to Mendes during the Santa Barbara International Film Festival, where he accepted the Kirk Douglas Award for Excellence in Film. In his speech, he gushed over his "dream" woman.

“Most importantly, I got to meet the girl of my dreams, Eva Mendes, and have two dream children," he said. “I dreamed one day of making movies, and now, movies have made my life a dream."

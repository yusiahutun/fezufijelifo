Saoirse Ronan shows off acne-hit complexion as she goes make-up free in New York

She always manages to draw admiring glances due to her flawless looks on the red carpet.

But Celtic goddess Saoirse Ronan proved she is human as the rest of us when she showed off her natural complexion, spots and all, as she stepped out in New York on Monday.

The 22-year-old Brooklyn star's acne was clearly visible as she ventured out to pick up some orange juice as she enjoyed a quiet day away from the celebrity rat race.

Au naturel: Saoirse Ronan did not try to hide her acne as she went on a make-up free stroll in NYC on Monday

The 22-year-old Irish beauty was looking in typically fine form in a leather biker jacket, skinny jeans and trendy beaded sandals.

But the most eye-catching element of the two-time Academy Award nominee's look was her brave decision to go virtually make-up free, despite spots being visable around her chin and cheeks.

It certainly did not detract from the County Dublin cutie's natural beauty, which shone through regardless of her little spot of bother.

But it does not look like sensible Saorise, who in March made her Broadway debut in a revival of The Crucible, is the type to be too caught up with her appearance.

Hell bent for leather: She was looking trendy in her biker jacket and black skinny jeans

She's got bottle: She clutched a container of juice as she revelled in her makeup free look

Now you're talking: She seemed to be having a great time on the blower as she strolled through the streets

Celtic goddess: Saoirse looked absolutely flawless in this emerald dress at the Academy Awards in February 

Indeed she said the thing that bothers her most is making sure she maintains her impressively high standards as an actress.

Talking about being nominated once again for an Oscar earlier this year, she said: 'That’s the worst thing for me. I’m a huge worrier as well. 

'The thing that I would worry about after being nominated again is, "How do I keep that up?" Not in relation to awards, but keeping up performances.

Multitasker: She made it look easy as she walked and talked at the same time while crossing the road

Saweetie sizzles in low-cut, figure-hugging dress as she makes her arrival at 4th of July bash

Saweetie looked stunning at the Fourth of July White Party at Nobu Malibu in Los Angeles on Tuesday.

The Icy Girl hitmaker, 30, showed off her hourglass figure in a tight-fitting gown with a low-cut neckline as she made her arrival to the star-studded affair walking hand-in-hand with her new beau and rapper, YG, 33.

Teamed with the figure-hugging dress with both halter and off-shoulder sleeves, she donned a pair of white sandal heels and carried a large quilted Chanel purse. 

For the celebratory event — which also saw the fateful reunion of Avril Lavigne and Tyga — she styled her raven black tresses in uniform beach waves that cascaded down her back.

The California native put on a glamorous display with her edges laid with diamonds as well as a makeup look featuring aqua-colored eyeshadow and dramatic eyeliner.

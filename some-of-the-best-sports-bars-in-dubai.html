6 Best Sports Bars in Dubai for Catching the Big Game

Are you in Dubai & searching for Sports bars near me? Well, you’re at the right place.  Sports bars in Dubai are an essential part of Dubai’s vibrant culture, offering a fantastic way to catch up on your favorite sports while enjoying a lively club atmosphere. Whether you are a proud football or basketball fan or just someone who loves the adrenaline rush of a thrilling game, Dubai’s sports bars have got your back. Throughout this blog.

we’ll explore some of the best sports bars in Dubai where you can enjoy a drink while soaking up some adrenaline.

1. Lock, Stock & Barrel

Topping our list of must-visit sports bars in Dubai is Lock, Stock & Barrel. This popular establishment has carved a niche for itself as the go-to destination for sports lovers. Lock and Barrel offers a massive variety of drinks to choose from. But it’s not just the drinks that steal the show – their food menu is equally remarkable, boasting a delectable range of dishes that will keep your taste buds dancing throughout the game. One of the highlights of this sports bar is the giant outdoor screen, which lets you enjoy the game under the dazzling Dubai night sky.

Address: 8th Floor, Grand Millennium Hotel Dubai – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Phone: +971 4 423 8309

Timings: Monday to Sunday – 4 pm to 3 am

2. Stars ‘N’ Bars

Situated in the heart of La Mer, Stars ‘N’ Bars is another gem in sports bars in Dubai scene. Offering mesmerizing views of the sea, this bar provides a relaxed setting for catching up with friends while enjoying the game. As you spend time in the warm camaraderie, don’t forget to taste their excellent selection of American-style dishes, perfectly complemented by a refreshing cold beer. What’s more, on weekends, a live DJ amps up the entertainment, adding a perfect groove to your sports-watching experience.

Address: La Mer North – 15 A Street – Jumeirah – Jumeirah 1 – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Phone: +971 4 349 9903

3. Kickers Sports Bar

If you find yourself in Dubai Sports City, Kickers sports bars in Dubai should be your go-to place. This sports haven boasts a spacious outdoor area, allowing you to bask in the pleasant Dubai weather while cheering for your favorite team. With over 15 screens scattered strategically across the bar, you won’t miss any crucial moments. Kickers’ drink menu is a delight for any connoisseur of beers, and their classic pub grub menu provides the perfect fuel for cheering your team to victory.

Address: Dubai Sports City – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Phone: +971 4 448 1001

Timings: Monday to Sunday- 12 pm to 2 am

4. McGettigan’s

Hailing from the renowned Irish bar chain, McGettigan’s in Dubai is a haven for those seeking an authentic Irish sports bar. The cozy and calm ambiance invites you to sit back and enjoy a pint of your favorite spirit while engrossed in the game. The outdoor terrace offers stunning views of the Dubai skyline, setting the stage for an unforgettable sports-watching session. Be sure to indulge in their delightful Irish favorites like fish and chips and bangers and mash for the complete experience.

Address: Next to Bonnington – Jumeirah Lake Towers – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Phone: +971 4 356 0470

Timings: Monday to Sunday- 12 pm to 3 pm

5. Brunswick Sports Club

Located in the Sheraton Hotel, Mall of the Emirates, Brunswick Sports Club offers a laid-back atmosphere that perfectly complements a casual get-together with friends. Their menu is a tribute to classic American fare, featuring juicy burgers and finger-licking wings that will keep you satiated while you cheer on your team on the big screens.

Address: Sheraton Mall of the Emirates Hotel, Dubai, Mall of The Emirates – Al Barsha – Al Barsha 1 – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Phone: +971 56 404 0685

6. Nezesaussi Grill

Nestled in the Address Dubai Marina, Nezesaussi Grill celebrates the sporting cultures of New Zealand, Australia, and South Africa. This lively sports bar boasts an impressive menu that reflects the diverse cuisines of these countries. With over 20 screens spread across the bar, you can keep track of all the action while enjoying mouthwatering dishes. The ambiance at Nezesaussi Grill exudes warmth and camaraderie, making it an ideal spot for watching sports with friends.

Address:  Address Dubai Marina Al Marsa Street, 66, Dubai Marina, 32923 – Dubai – United Arab Emirates

Phone: +971 4 436 7777

Timings: Monday to Sunday – 1 pm to 1 am

Final Words!

sports bars in Dubai offer a dynamic range of experiences to suit every sports enthusiast’s preferences. Whether it’s the bustling ambiance of Lock, Stock & Barrel, or the relaxed charm of McGettigan’s, each sports bar has its unique charm. With an impressive variety of drinks and mouthwatering dishes to accompany the games, these sports bars create the perfect backdrop for unforgettable sports-watching sessions with friends. Embrace the thrill of the game and Create Cherished memories as you cheer your team to victory amidst the company of fellow sports aficionados. Head to one of these stellar sports bars and let the games begin!

10 Spring Handbag Trends Worth Investing In

Adding the latest spring bag trends to our accessories rotation is one of the little treats that keeps life fun. A pop of color, whimsical appliqués, or jewelry-inspired straps can make even the cloudiest of days feel full of sunshine. The latest spring bag trends have something for everyone, even folks who prefer their accessories subtle, all-black, and streamlined.

In fact, this spring, the most runway-spotted and on-trend styles are more diverse than ever before. Instead of a singular silhouette taking over, this spring's trends are refreshingly varied. From classic silhouettes with tasteful modern touches to throwback styles reimagined and redesigned, the 10 bags trends ahead are a breath of fresh (spring) air.  

Bold Gold Hardware

Unless you're a buckle bag devotee, stunning hardware might not be on your list of bag must-haves, but this spring bag trend is set to change all that. From clever clips to high-shine locks and keys, bold gold hardware is a trend likely to appeal to both trend-chasers and fans of timeless accessories.

Flashy Florals

Florals for spring aren't groundbreaking, but they can certainly be statement-making. This season, designers have delivered a garden of earthly delights thanks to colorful and metallic appliqués that add enchantment without abandoning sophistication.

Wristlet Redux

The humble wristlets of the early aughts have taken a new form for spring. No longer minimal and wallet-like, this season's wristlets feature slouchy organic shapes, bold bracelet-style chains, and eye-catching textures like supple leather and summer-ready raffia.

Two-Tone Texture

Contrast, but make it chic. This spring, designers have set aside color-blocking in favor of two-tone bags and purses enriched with multiple textures. By far the most creative spring bag trend we've come across, this style of trending bag brings together raffia, resin, canvas, and leather for truly unique takes on classic silhouettes.

Chic Charms

Chic charm adornments are another trend that allows you to show off your personal style as well as your taste level. From nostalgic baubles to Y2K whimsy and metallic fringe that evokes a fine jewelry vibe, there are plenty of ways to bring this trend into your accessory rotation. For fashion girlies on a budget, consider shopping for designer bag charms to add to your favorite crossbody or top-handle purse.

Knotty by Nature

One of the more streamlined spring bag trends, knotted top handle bags are an ode to quiet luxury and minimalist statement-making. Sleek or slouchy silhouettes are elevated by knot details set on straps and top handles proving that trend-chasing can be sophisticated and subtle, rather than flashy.

Chain Gang

Shiny chains are frequently spotted on crossbody bags, but they're shifting into the spotlight in this bag trend. Instead of delivering straightforward functionality, many of this spring's trendiest bags feature chains as adornments. The outcome of this shift from function to form? Bags that add a metallic pop to any outfit and catch the eye without being overtly attention-grabbing.

Big Bamboo Energy

If you love a classic bag with a twist, this is the spring bag trend for you. Subtle bamboo details put a unique spin on classic top-handle and satchel bag styles without detracting from these silhouettes' timeless feel.

Top Notch Top Handles

We called it during New York Fashion Week and this spring's bag trends have proven us right: top-handle bags are the moment. Thanks to designers like Loewe, Etro, and Tory Burch, you don't have to sacrifice space to get in on this trend. Unlike the tiny top-handle bags of previous seasons, there's plenty of room for all your essentials in this style.

Full Transparency

Don't get it twisted — this season's trending clear bags are a world away from concert-approved PVC fanny packs. Sure, full transparency is key, but this spring's see-through styles also feature subtle tinted colors and elevated adornments.

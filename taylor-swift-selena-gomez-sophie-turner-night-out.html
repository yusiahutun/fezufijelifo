Taylor Swift, Selena Gomez, Sophie Turner Have Girls' Night

Taylor Swift stepped out for a big girls’ night in New York City Saturday.

Each celebrity in her star-studded crew—including Selena Gomez, Sophie Turner, Gigi Hadid, Cara Delevingne, and Brittany Mahomes—brought her own fashionable flair to dinner at NoHo’s famed sushi restaurant BondST.

The “Anti-Hero” singer perfected another autumn preppy look with a black long sleeve top, a pleated checkered miniskirt cinched at the waist with a black leather belt, black thigh-high boots, and a black purse. She completed the look with a dainty gold necklace, gold earrings, and her signature black winged eyeliner and red lip.

Gomez looked cozy in a peachy knit turtleneck dress, tan belt, and black flats. She accessorized with chunky gold hoops and a black Yves Saint Laurent bag; she wore her hair in a slicked-back bun. Gomez's presence at the dinner came days after she and Swift enjoyed a an intimate girls’ night out in L.A. with friend Zoë Kravitz.

For the weekend outing, Turner went with an all-blue getup composed of a checkered jacket over a dark top, light wash jeans, and black pointed-toe pumps. She carried a brown Louis Vuitton purse and wore her hair down. The “Game of Thrones” actress has been seen out with Swift several times in the past few months, including at a dinner with Blake Lively and Mahomes in October.

Mahomes, wife of Kansas City Chiefs player Patrick Mahomes, also stunned in blue, wearing a blue corset with a criss-cross neck detail and matching pants. She switched things up with a white coat, white pumps, and white leather purse. She and Swift have developed a friendship since the singer started dating Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce.

Hadid looked chic in a black blazer dress, black knee high boots, and a slicked-back bun. The model was spotted in her BFF’s merch on Friday, sporting the 1989 (Taylor’s Version) cardigan.

Finally, Delevingne kept things fun with a fluffy black-and-white checkered bucket hat, black shearling lined leather jacket, and black leather and blue denim chaps by Stella McCartney.

'He was begging to marry me': Brad Womack's former fiancée claims The Bachelor pursued her weeks before the show

Old flame: Laurel Kagay claims that The Bachelor star Brad Womack proposed to her just before filming the new series in September last year

The women in this season's run of The Bachelor have been warned off leading man Brad Womack by his former fiancée.

Laurel Kagay claims that Brad begged her to marry him just weeks before going on the show.

The spa owner from Austin, Texas said Brad last proposed to her in September - just before going off to film the show.

The couple had been in a stormy on-off relationship for eight years and Laurel told Us Magazine that there's nothing Brad has said on the show that she hasn't heard before.

'I'm sure the girl he picked would want to know that he was proposing to another woman right before he started taping the show. I would want to know', Laurel said.

She added: 'Before she decides to marry Brad, she needs to know where his head and his heart are'.

Even after Brad's 2007 stint on The Bachelor, Laurel said he contacted her to try and make their relationship work once again.

Brad had suffered a backlash when he refused to choose between DeAnna Pappas and Jenni Croft after jilting both of them in the finals.

Last summer Brad sent Laurel a message using his nick-name for her, Roo, which is
short for kangaroo.

'You're my future, Roo. Nothing is going to change that. Laurel, I'm serious about  this. Please marry me?,' he sent by text.

He sent a follow up message saying: 'The executive producers from Bachelor called. Want me to do the show again. Haven't discussed $ but it would be a lot'.

Not over: In an exclusive interview with Us Weekly, Laurel says Brad is living a double life by pursuing the women in the show while still having feelings for her

Over the next few weeks Brad sent Laurel numerous text messages saying, 'I miss the hell out of you', and 'Just wish you would let me in your life'.

Then she said Brad was gone and the next time she saw his face was on screen in the latest season of the hit ABC reality dating show.

But she did continue to hear from him in birthday messages, which Laurel ignored and then a final message on November 2 which read: 'Never heard from you. Hope you are doing OK. Thinking of you'.

It was the last time Brad contacted her.

He said he has had three years of intensive therapy since the last show to overcome his commitment issues.

Desperate: Brad has become close with numerous girls in the house including Michelle Money who claims she is the woman for him

'It was probably closer to three months', Laurel claims who briefly attended couples therapy with Brad in 2006.'

She said he went twice again in 2009 and 2010 but only for a few weeks each time. '

'He never stuck with it', Laurel said.

Brad has been romancing several women on the show and some have already seemed to have captured his heart.

Southern girl Emily Maynard has upset the other girls in the house for the amount of attention she has received from Brad.

While Chantal O'Brien has been open about her feelings for the 37-year-old bachelor and has had frequent emotional conversations with Brad.

But Laurel claims all the tears and the drama that has happened in the house will be a turn off for her ex.

'I knew the comforting was act, because he hates that!', Laurel said of Brad wiping the girls' tears.

Tierney Stout Launches New Music Strategy &amp; Branding Company Saus Haus

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – SAUS HAUS, a newly launched cross-functional music strategy and branded agency, is making waves in the industry. Founded by Tierney Stout, a seasoned professional with over 20 years of experience in the music industry, the company is dedicated to developing, brokering, refining, and driving thoughtful and integrated artist partnerships and concept creations.

With an impressive client list that includes Dr. Martens, Levi’s, and Marshall, SAUS HAUS, a female-owned and run business, is poised to make a significant impact in the music partnership and brand space. Stout emphasizes the universal passion for music and the company’s specialization in strategically aligning the right brands and artists in meaningful partnerships.

“Passion for music is universal. Regardless of the type of music, it is what we all have in common. SAUS HAUS specializes in leveraging consumers’ love for music and strategically aligning the right brands and artists in meaningful partnerships. We speak the language of the consumer, the brand, and the artists, and we are able to tell a compatible and consistent story that makes brands relatable and desirable,” says Stout.

Stout has created campaigns, events, and product collaborations with major artists, including Anderson .Paak, Little Simz, Tierra Whack, Lil Wayne, YUNGBLUD, MUNA, Metallica, A$AP Rocky, Foo Fighters, Mac Miller, IDLES, JID, Denzel Curry, KennyHoopla, ScHoolboy Q, The Linda Lindas and more.

SAUS HAUS offers a comprehensive music and data-forward strategy for brands, covering aspects from product seeding to key opinion leaders and tastemakers, retail playlisting, seasonal and retail campaign casting, and live performance curation to artist collaborations and partnerships. The company utilizes consumer insights, streaming data, social analytics, and a thorough evaluation of the competitive landscape to build a roadmap that makes music an influential and dynamic aspect of a brand’s image, expanding its audience with revitalized cultural substance.

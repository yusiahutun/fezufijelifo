Tim McGraw Recognizes National Daughters Day With Stunning Photo

If ever there were a country superstar who could not let National Daughter' Day pass without recognition, that artist would be Tim McGraw, who has three gorgeous girls of his own that he made sure to celebrate on Wednesday (Sept. 25).

McGraw shared a stunning photo of his three beautiful daughters Gracie, Maggie and Audrey, captioning it, "There is nothing as beautifully inspiring as being the father of 3 remarkable, talented and self-confident young women!" The girls all share a family resemblance, but have undeniably grown into their own individual looks over the years.

The singer and his wife, Faith Hill, are just about finished with their nest, as Gracie is 22 and Maggie 21. Youngest Audrey will turn 18 in December and will be done with high school soon. The couple married in 1996.

McGraw spoke with Taste of Country about becoming empty-nesters once his third daughter leaves for college (which is coming up rapidly), as well as other practical parenting matters such as driving his older daughter to L.A. to begin her acting career. You can watch the full interview below.

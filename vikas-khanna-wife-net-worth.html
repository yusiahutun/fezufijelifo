Is Vikas Khanna Married-Wife? His Career, Net Worth 2023, Dating Rumor

Vikas Khanna is an Indian filmmaker, cookbook writer, chef as well as judge of the Television Show, Master Chef India, and Master Chef Junior. Similarly, Vikas has judged Master Chef Season 2, 3, 4, 5, and Master Junior.

Where was Vikas Khanna Born?

Vikas Khanna was born on 14th November 1971, in Amritsar, India. As of 2023, he is 52 years old and his horoscope is Scorpio. He is the son of Davinder Khanna (father) and Bindu Khanna (mother). Vikas is the second child born to the Khanna family.

He has a brother, Nishant Khanna, and a sister, Radhika Khanna. Vikas holds an Indian nationality and belongs to Asian ethnical background. Khanna has a belief in the Hindu religion.

Where did Vikas Khanna Study?

From the early phase, he was interested in cooking and planning to make it his professional career with his strong mentality. He used to learn cooking from his grandmother with a variety of dishes with new tastes and choices. He completed his education in the year 1991 at the Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration.

Does Vikas Khanna have a Wife?

Vikas is unmarried yet. He has been linked up with Shipra Khanna who is the winner of the second season of Master Chef India. He might start dating in the near future.

How much is Vikas Khanna’s Net Worth?

Vikas has maintained an impressive net worth from his successful career. He is the goodwill ambassador for Smile Foundation and has around $5 million as of 2023. He earned that sum of money from his career as a chef.

What is the Height of Vikas Khanna?

Vikas stands at a tall height of 5 feet 11 inches and weighs around 82 kg. His eyes and hair are black in color.

Khanna’s Career Started for Leela Group of Hotels

  • Vikas Khanna began his professional career by joining the Leela Group of Hotels and then worked with the Welcome group and Taj Group of Hotels.
  • After working at various places, he finally started his own restaurant, Junoon in New York in the year 2010.

Khanna’s Books

He is also a famous cookbook author who has penned down some of the most exclusive cooking books the world has ever witnessed.

Among all the books Vikas has penned down some of the books which received worldwide recognition such as Modern Indian Cooking, Savour Mumbai, World Feast – My Favourite Kitchen, Shaken & Stirred- A Non-Alcoholic Drinks Book, and many more.
He rose to the media spotlight after he penned down a 1200-page book, UTSAV- A Culinary Epic of Indian Festivals. He has hosted some cook shows such as Master Chef India, Twist of Taste, Master Chef Junior, and many more.

Furthermore, Vikas has worked for various fundraising events and has established his own foundation, SAKIV (South Asian Kid’s Infinite Vision). Also, he has been appointed as the Goodwill Ambassador of a foundation, Smile Foundation.

FAQs of Vikas Khanna

Does Vikas Khanna have a disability?

Yes, Vikas has a disability. He was born with a leg deformity. Due to this, he was not able to run until he was 13 years old.

Did Vikas Khanna work for Gordon Ramsay?

No, Vikas did not work with Gordon Ramsay. But Gordan invited Vikas to help revamp the Indian restaurant Purnima in New York.

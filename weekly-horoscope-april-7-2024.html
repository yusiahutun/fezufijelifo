The Most Intense Week of 2024

Your horoscope for the week of April 7 to 13 predicts the most transformative and chaotic energy of the entire year. If you’re wondering why you feel completely out of your wits, know that it’s just not you. Eclipse season has reached its peak, and whether you’re ready or not, the cosmos are a hard-launching new era.

When I say this week will be intense, that’s almost an understatement. It all begins with a total solar eclipse in Aries on April 8, which bring forth a bold burst of change that speeds you up toward your ultimate destiny. Get ready for next level emotional tension, as this eclipse lands at the same degree as Chiron—the wounded healer—which means you’ll will be traversing the most vulnerable regions of your heart. However, it will also give you the tools to heal what needs healing, forcing you to reckon with what remains unresolved.

As powerhouse Mars joins forces with disciplinary Saturn on April 10, you may feel like you’re stuck between a rock and a hard place. And as this conjunction lands in confounding and imaginative Pisces, you may feel even more confused about how to proceed. Pressures are mounting, as though you’re taking energetic beatings from all sides. However, this energy will help you tap into your resilience and forge something as impenetrable as iron. Remember, we become our strongest after we realize how much more we’re capable of surviving and defeating.

As if you needed another kicker, Mercury is currently still retrograde, and because it takes place in Aries, it’s making us all want to fight and argue. And as the Aries sun joins forces with Mercury retrograde on April 11, this frustrating and disorienting energy will be emphasized, as Mercury will technically be “combust”. But not before a brilliant Mercury cazimi clears away the fog, activating powerful insights and psychic downloads. Get ready to cleanse your whole perspective of the past, present, and future this week.
